Personal Data Protection

Provision of Personal Data

The provision of the requested data is voluntary.
Personal data collected
                will be used in matters relating to the operations of the Company and may
                be disclosed to third party organisations which assist in the Company's
                operations.
Failure to provide the data requested may mean that the
                Company is unable to supply the relevant services.
Such data may also,
                unless the customer in writing requests otherwise, be used for the purpose
                of direct marketing by the Company itself.
