This tracking information may be collected through the use of cookies.
A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your browser.
A website may use cookies to recognize repeat users or track web usage behavior.
Cookies take up minimal room on your device and cannot damage your device’s files.
Cookies work by assigning a number to the user that has no meaning outside of the assigning website.
