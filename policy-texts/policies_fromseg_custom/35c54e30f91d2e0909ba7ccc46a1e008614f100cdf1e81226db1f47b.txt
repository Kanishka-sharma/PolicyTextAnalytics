Privacy 
              Policy
At Fender Musical Instruments 
              Corporation, your privacy is important to us, and we would like 
              to provide you with answers to the following questions regarding 
              our privacy policy.
I.
==========
What information 
              does FMIC collect about me when I visit www.fender.com and how is 
              that information used?
For the purpose of updating 
              and improving our web site to serve our customers more effectively, 
              we collect general information about you when you visit fender.com, 
              including your domain name, the type and version of browser and 
              operating system that you use, how much time you spend browsing 
              our site and which pages you visit.
==========
However, unless you voluntarily 
              provide personal information to us, such as your name or email address, 
              we do not collect such information about you.
We use personal information 
              provided by you only to perform services that you request, such 
              as adding your email address to a mailing list or answering your 
              technical support questions.
II.
Does Fender share 
              any information about me with third parties?
No.
We will use our 
              best efforts not to transfer information that personally identifies 
              you to anyone else without your knowledge and approval at the time 
              that you submit such information to us.
III.
Will I receive 
              unsolicited email as a result of visiting fender.com?
No.
We will only contact 
              you in connection with services that you request, such as confirming 
              that you have been added to a subscription list or responding to 
              your email queries.
