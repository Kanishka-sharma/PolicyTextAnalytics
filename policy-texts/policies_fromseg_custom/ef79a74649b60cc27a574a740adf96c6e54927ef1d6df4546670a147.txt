As explained in our Advertising Technology Privacy Policy, Magnite does not sell the personal information it collects.
Nevertheless, our clients may use our technology to buy or sell personal information as defined by California law.
If you wish to opt out of the use of your personal information for interest-based advertising purposes and these potential sales under California law, you may do so by following the instructions below.
==========
Note that because these opt outs are specific to the device or browser on which they are exercised, you will need to opt out on every browser and device that you use.
There are many ways to opt out of receiving targeted advertising that our clients may conduct using our technology.
Note that different devices use different identifiers and different technologies, so you must opt out separately from each browser and each device to which you would like the opt out to apply.
If you opt out of our targeted advertising, we will no longer process the User Information described above from the opted-out device for targeted advertising purposes.
Display
If you would like to opt out of the use of our technology to show ads that are targeted to your interests based on your web browsing activity on this browser, click the box below and then click “OK ”:
When you enable this choice, we will place a cookie on your device that is unique to the browser and device you use to opt out.
If you change browsers or devices, or delete the cookies on your device, you will need to opt out again.
You also can set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set and to block most cookies, including ours.
==========
Mobile
Your device may give you the ability to opt out of the use of information about the apps you use in order to serve you ads that are targeted to your interests.
You may stop the collection of location information by particular apps or from your device as a whole at any time by changing the preferences on your mobile device.
For more information on how to change your preferences, please see http://www.networkadvertising.org/mobile-choice.
==========
Internet Connected Televisions
Your Internet connected device (e.g. a smart TV or connected through another device) may give you the ability to opt out of the use of information for advertising purposes.
To determine if your Internet connected device has these options, please visit your device’s settings menu.
For more information on how to change your preferences, please see https://www.networkadvertising.org/internet-connected-tv-choices/.
