A cookie may also
    convey information about how you browse the Service to us.
A cookie does not collect personal information about you.
A persistent cookie
    remains on your hard drive after you close your browser.

A session cookie is temporary and disappears after you close your browser.

    properly if the ability to accept cookies is disabled.
