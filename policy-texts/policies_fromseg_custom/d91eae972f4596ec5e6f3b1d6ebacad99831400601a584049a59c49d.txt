Cookies
MRP’s Web sites may save information on your system in the form of a “cookie” to access secure information or to better track usage of our Web sites.
There is no personally identifiable information stored in the cookie.
A cookie is either a small text file that is stored on your hard drive, or some information that is stored in memory until you close your browser.
Web pages that write and read cookies can only interact with the cookie files(s) for your browser and a cookie can only be accessed by the same domain server that sent it.
This saves you from having to re-enter the same information as you access secure information.
==========
Session ID Cookies
We may use session ID cookies and persistent cookies.
We use session cookies to make it easier for a visitor to navigate the Sites we operate.
A session ID cookie expires when a visitor closes their browser.
A persistent cookie remains on a hard drive for an extended period of time.
==========
Visitors can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in their Internet browser’s “help” file.
We set a persistent cookie to store a visitor’s password, so they don’t have to enter it more than once.
Persistent cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on this Site.
If a visitor rejects cookies, they may still use the Site, but their ability to use some Site areas, such as contests or surveys, will be limited.
