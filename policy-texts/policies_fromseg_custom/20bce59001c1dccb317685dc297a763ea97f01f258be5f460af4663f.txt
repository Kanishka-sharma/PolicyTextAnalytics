Cookies
The responsible entity uses cookies in order to design their website and to allow certain functions for website use.
Cookies are small text files which are stored on the user’s browser.
Most of the cookies used by the responsible entity will be automatically deleted at the end of the browser session (Known as session cookies).
Other cookies remain on the user’s computer and allow the responsible entity to recognize the user when they visit the site again (Known as persistent cookies).
A user is free to reject cookies, as long as their browser allows this.
