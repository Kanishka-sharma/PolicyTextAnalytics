In order to operate our business, we may source, use, and otherwise process Personal Data about different types of data subjects, including:
• Visitors to publishers’ websites and users of digital properties: Consumers that visit and otherwise interact with websites, mobile apps, and other Internet-connected properties (e.g., Internet-connected TVs) that advertisers (or companies acting on behalf of advertisers) use to deliver, measure, or track advertising and obtain other insights.

• Visitors to our premises: Individuals who visit any MiQ physical facility or office.

These segments generally involve aggregated groups of users, typically thousands or hundreds of thousands of users who may share common characteristics.
