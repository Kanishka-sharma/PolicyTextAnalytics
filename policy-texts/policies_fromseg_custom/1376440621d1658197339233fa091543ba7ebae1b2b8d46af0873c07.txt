Use of cookies
A cookie is a character string emitted by a Web server for the attention of a browser, the latter re-emits this when a request is made to the site.
The cookie enables the site to recognise the browsers of users who have already consulted or registered on the site.
You can control how websites use cookies by configuring the privacy settings within your browser (please refer to your browser help function to learn more about cookie controls).
Note that if you disable cookies entirely, some websites and applications may not function properly.
The cookies we set
