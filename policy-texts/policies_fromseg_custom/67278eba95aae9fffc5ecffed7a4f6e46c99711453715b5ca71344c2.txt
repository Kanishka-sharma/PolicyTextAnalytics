Cookies
We collect information using “cookie” technology.
Cookies are small packets of data that a website stores on your computer’s or mobile device’s hard drive so that your computer will “remember” information about your visit.
We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to help us collect information.
If you do not want us to place a cookie on your hard drive, you may be able to turn that feature off on your computer or mobile device.
Please consult your Internet browser’s documentation for information on how to do this and how to delete persistent cookies.
However, if you decide not to accept cookies from us, the Website may not function properly.
