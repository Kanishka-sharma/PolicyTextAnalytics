Information Collected and its Uses
Information You Provide
When you sign up for a Hatena.com account, we ask you for Personal Information (such as your name, email address, and an account password).
The information we collect is used to provide you with a better experience and to improve the quality of your experience on the Site.
If a child under 13 wishes to set up a Hatena account, we will ask his or her parent to provide us with verifiable parental consent to the collection, use and processing of information from such child.
==========
Cookies
We use cookies on the Site.
Cookies are small text files that the Site places on your hard drive whenever you visit the Site.
These files identify your computer and record your preferences and other data about your visit so that when you return to the Site, it knows who you are and can personalize your visit.
We use cookies to improve the quality of our service, to confirm when a User has logged into the flipnote service, to measure the number of unique Registered Users of the flipnote service, and for Registered User authentication.
==========
Advertisers on our Site may also set cookies.
Hatena has no control over such cookies, which are bound by the privacy policies of such parties.
The Site also utilizes third-party cookies and image files (web beacons) in order to anonymously track and analyze website usage and activity.
This information is compiled and analyzed on an aggregate basis.
==========
Nintendo DSi Users
For Users who access the Hatena services supporting the Nintendo DSi, Hatena automatically collects the Nintendo DSi machine ID (“MAC ID”) that is being used to access the Flipnote service, DSi Username, User date of birth as well as the User Flipnote Hatena friend list and MAC IDs of the Users’ Flipnote Hatena friend list members.
Log Information
When you access the Site, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website.
These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request, and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.
==========
Log information is used to analyze the ways Users are utilizing the flipnote services, to identify confirmation, and to investigate suspected violation of the Rules.
User Communications
When you send email or other communications to Hatena, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests, and improve the Flipnote services.
User Generated Content
Remember that when you upload a flipnote to the Site it may be viewed by people all over the world.
You should take extra care not to include any personally identifiable information about yourself in a flipnote or any comments you make on the Site.
==========
Hatena is not responsible for voluntarily posted information, which may be collected and used by others.

Information Security
Your User Registration Personal Information can only be viewed by you after correctly entering your Username and Password.
Except for children under 13 whose parents or guardians may view, access and update their information, other Users or a third party cannot view this information in order to protect your privacy.
SSL code is used to transmit data you submit to the Site in order to protect sensitive data during transmission.
==========
Except for authorized personnel, Hatena employees are prohibited from accessing User Registration Personal Information.
Hatena employees are bound by a confidentiality agreement, and must observe the Security Policy set by Hatena.

As a general rule, Hatena allows Users to view, edit, or remove their own User Registration Personal Information by going to My Hatena after login.
