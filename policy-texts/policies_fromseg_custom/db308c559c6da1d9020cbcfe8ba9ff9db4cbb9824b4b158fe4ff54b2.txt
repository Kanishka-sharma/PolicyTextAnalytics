Information We Collect
Our primary goal in collecting personal information from you is to provide you with a smooth and efficient experience while using Yonja.
This allows us to provide services and features that most likely meet your needs, and to customize our service to make your experience easier and quicker.
==========
When you join Yonja using our online registration process, we collect an email address, user name, and password as your Personal Identifier Info.
Your user name is used throughout Yonja to identify you to other Yonja Members.
==========
Therefore, you should be aware that if you use your real name as your user name, this information would be visible to others on the site.
If you use your email address as your user name, this information would be visible to others on the site.
You may also provide Yonja additional information that is displayed publicly on the Site in your profile.
Other than the profile information submitted by our users, and except as noted herein, Yonja does not disclose any personally identifying information without your permission.
Except as set forth in this Policy, at no time will we share your Personal Identifier Info with anyone, other than those users with whom you communicate through Yonja.
==========
Yonja facilitates communication among users through our on-site Yonja private messaging system.
Messages sent via Yonja private messaging are identified only by your user name, and we do not reveal either user's personal email address unless it is part of their Profile.
We use your email address to contact you regarding administrative notices, alerts and communications relevant to your use of Yonja.
If you do not wish to receive these communications, you have the ability to stop receiving these communications by unchecking the Notification Parameter options on the Profile page.
==========
You will receive administrative emails that are related to the primary usage and functionality of Yonja.
You may not opt-out of administrative emails.
You can review, update and change your Personal Identifier Info -- including your user name, email address, and  password -- by clicking on the [Edit my profile] Information link on your Profile page.
==========
Yonja and our users do not tolerate spam.
Therefore, without limiting the foregoing, you are not licensed to send spam.
To report Yonja related spam to Yonja, please report the user through the "Report User" link to Yonja or contact Yonja through our Contact Us window.
==========
We use information we maintain about you, and other information we obtain from your current and past activities on the Site, to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems and enforce our User Agreement.
At times, we may look at your account information and transaction history to identify problems or resolve disputes.
==========
Moreover, such information is never completely removed from our databases due to technical constraints and the fact we back up our systems.
Therefore, our user's Personal Identifier Info may not be completely removed from our databases in response to user requests.
==========
We also receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our Web site.
Yonja automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, Yonja cookie information and the page you requested.
Yonja Cookies are small amounts of data sent to a user's browser from the Yonja Web servers and stored on a user's computer's hard drive.
Yonja uses cookies to track the pages on the Site that our users have visited.
This allows us to collect aggregate information about usage on the Site.
==========
Yonja may uses cookies to deliver content specific to users' interests, and to keep track of users' user names and passwords so that users do not have to reenter them during a session.
If you choose not to accept cookies, you will be unable to use the Yonja website.
==========
Generally our service automatically collects usage information, such as the numbers and frequency of visitors to our site and its components, similar to TV ratings that indicate how many people watched a particular show.
Yonja only uses this data in aggregate form, that is, as a statistical measure, and not in a manner that would identify you personally.
Yonja uses your IP addresses to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer the Site.
==========
Generally, IP addresses tell us the location from which a person is connecting to us, what service provider the person is using, and which parts of the Site the User is visiting.
We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable.
==========
This means that your session will be tracked, but you will remain anonymous.
We reserve the right to track IP addresses for the purposes of fraud prevention, and we reserve the right to release IP addresses to legal authorities.
If you send Yonja email correspondence, or if other users or other third parties send us email or other correspondence regarding a particular user's activities or postings on the Site, Yonja may collect such information.
