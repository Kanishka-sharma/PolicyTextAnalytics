FreeServers Privacy Policy
Freeservers 
                  collects only aggregate information of visitors to it's network 
                  of sites, including the domain name, visited pages, referring 
                  URLs, and other publicly available information.
Freeservers 
                  uses this information to help improve its web site and services, 
                  and to customize the content of its pages for each individual 
                  consumer.
==========
Freeservers also works with Flycast, a third party 
                  that serves ads to this site.
To find out more about how Flycast 
                  manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving 
                  ads on this site, please go to http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html.
==========
Freeservers 
                  does not sell or commercially make available specific information 
                  about its users or members other than in aggregate form, except 
                  in cooperation with law enforcement bodies in regards to content 
                  violations or violations of applicable laws.
Freeservers maintains 
                  a database of member information which is used only for internal 
                  purposes such as advertising, collections efforts, technical 
                  support and notifying members of changes or enhancements to 
                  the Freeservers service, new services and products that may 
                  be of interest to them, and special discounts available to Freeservers 
                  members.
==========
Any Freeservers 
                  member may cancel their membership at any time by going to http://www.freeservers.com/cgi-bin/util/deleteaccount.
When an account is cancelled, all user information is removed 
                  from the Freeservers system related to that account, other than 
                  that in aggregate form.
Questions or problems regarding this 
                  policy may be sent to abuse@freeservers.com
==========
