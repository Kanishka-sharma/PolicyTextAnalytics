Cookies
When you visit the eCollege website we may send you a “cookie”.
A cookie is a small data or text file that is stored on your computer to enable us to recognize you each time you visit the eCollege website and to help us analyze how users use the eCollege website.
The information generated by the cookie about your use of the eCollege website (including your IP address) will be transmitted and stored for analysis.
eCollege will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the eCollege website, compiling reports on website activity for our website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.
eCollege may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties collect, process or store the information on eCollege 's behalf.
eCollege will not associate your IP address with any other data held by eCollege.
You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of the eCollege website.
