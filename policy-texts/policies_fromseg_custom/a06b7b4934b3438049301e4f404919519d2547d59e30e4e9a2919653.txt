This document includes all advertising on our network of websites, as well as across other internal sites, and products and services that are offered by Scenestealer (referred to as “Scenestealer”, “we”, “us”, or “our”).
This policy was created to let you know what information we collect when you visit our websites and when we serve advertisements, why we collect it, and how it is used.
This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Scenestealer does not own or control, or to people that Scenestealer does not employ.
What information is collected?
Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
Scenestealer does not collect any personally identifiable information.
Non Personally Identifiable Information (NPII)
Scenestealer collects non personally identifiable information on you how interact with our websites or advertisements we have served.
This may include social sharing, links you have clicked, searches you have made or media you have interacted with.
==========
How is this information used?
The non-personally identifiable data that Scenestealer and/or its third-party advertising technology vendors collect are used to better target advertisements and offers to consumers across the different websites they visit within the Scenestealer network of websites.
Scenestealer may also use the non-personally identifiable data to better target content to consumers in an effort to create a personally relevant experience for each consumer.
This data is also used to predict responses to advertisements, and to help determine which ads perform best and which content is most appropriate for different consumers.
In addition, the data may be combined with information collected from other sources so that we may better serve consumers with advertisements and offers that may be of interest to them.
==========
We may also share non-personally identifiable data with our subsidiaries and third-party partners for similar use.
Scenestealer may retain non-personally identifiable data for up to 3 months.
After 3 months, we may store the non-personally identifiable data in aggregate form for up to 18 months.

Additional notices and disclousures
Children’s Privacy
Scenestealer is very sensitive to the issue of children’s privacy.
Scenestealer’s websites, products, and services are neither developed for, nor directed at, children under thirteen years of age.
If you believe your child has provided Scenestealer with personally identifiable data, or registered at one of Scenestealer’s websites, and you would like to have the data removed, please contact us.
Data Transfer
Scenestealer is required to tell you if it transfers or intends to transfer personally identifiable data which it holds about you to countries outside the European Economic Area (“EEA”), where the privacy laws may not be deemed as protective as those in your jurisdiction.
Changes to Our Privacy Policy
This privacy policy is subject to change without notice.
We may change our privacy policy at any time by posting a new version of it on our website.
Each version of this privacy policy will be identified by its effective date.
==========
Consumer Communication
If you contact us, we may keep a record of that communication.
We may retain emails or other communications sent to us from consumers in order to help process inquiries, respond to requests, and improve our general service levels.
