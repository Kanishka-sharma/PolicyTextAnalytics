What personal information is collected?
Electronic Arts only collects personal information from our online visitors on a voluntary basis and only during (1) contest registration, (2) warranty registration, (3) player match up services, (4) registration for games or special game-specific event participation, (5) marketing surveys and email campaigns, (6) registration for membership on our sites; and (7) when you order products on our Online Store.
Personal information collected may include your name, e-mail address, phone number, home address and birth date.
We do not require this information to obtain access to any of our sites; however, you will not be able to access areas that require registration.
EA's Online Store
Visitors purchasing goods and services through our online store will need to provide a valid credit card number and expiration date, in addition to name, mailing address(es), phone number and e-mail address.

We recognize a special obligation to protect personal information obtained from young children.

What about cookies?
Certain of our sites use cookies to keep track of your shopping cart and make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly.
Also, we use cookies to deliver content specific to your interest and for other purposes.
(Cookies are small files downloaded to your computer to track movements within web sites.)
==========
Certain of our sites use an outside ad company to display ads.
These ads may contain cookies.
Cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.
Some consumer data is shared with the advertising companies.
==========
Most browsers are automatically set to accept cookies.
You can disable cookies or set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent.
However some areas will not function properly if you do so.

What happens to the information provided?
Electronic Arts does not disclose any personally identifiable information about registered children under 18 years of age to third parties, regardless of parental consent.
If you register to become a member of EA.com, and you are 18 years of age or older, your email address is also provided to our partner, America Online, Inc. and you are automatically registered for an AOL Instant Messenger (AIM) Account.
AOL will email you directly concerning your AIM account.
Because we cannot control the activities of third parties, such as AOL, we cannot guarantee that they will adhere to the same privacy and security practices as Electronic Arts.
We encourage you to review AOL's privacy policy, found at www.aol.com.
