Some of our site’s pages utilize “cookies” and other tracking technologies to collect information and to tailor our site to the needs and interests of our customers.
A “cookie” is a small text file that is transferred from our site’s servers to the computer’s hard drive, and uniquely identifies the user’s browser.
Cookies can only be read by the server that sent them, and they cannot read data from the hard drive.
