Use of Cookies
Cookies are pieces of data stored on a user’s hard drive that contain information about a user.
In the case of secure content access, if your company is a client of Miaozhen Systems, we use a cookie that contains your login information, so we can ensure that client-specific information is secure from unauthorized access.
We utilize an encrypted cookie to track client access to client-specific secure content.
==========
These cookies are used only for this purpose and are intended to be used only by our Web site.
Please note that you can view and delete these cookies using the features of your browser.
==========
How to opt out of the Miaozhen Systems cookie
You can opt out of the Miaozhen Systems cookie at any time from HERE.
If you choose to opt out, The Miaozhen Systems opt-out cookie will be placed in your browser to identify you as someone who has elected to opt out.
As long as your browser retains the Miaozhen Systems opt-out cookie, Miaozhen Systems won’t serve new Miaozhen Systems cookies to your browser.
When you get a new computer, install a new browser, erase or otherwise alter your browser’s cookie file (including upgrading certain browsers) you may also clear the cookies in your browser, including the Miaozhen Systems opt-out cookie.
You need to opt out again to recover your opt-out cookie.
Alternatively, you can simply delete your Miaozhen Systems cookies with your browser's cookie management tools.
To explore these tools, look in the "preferences" or "options" section of your browser's menu.

You can delete the cookies under this domain at any time.
