Use of Cookies


A cookie is simply a small piece of information that a web server asks a Visitor’s browser to store on the Visitor’s local computer, and then later their browser presents this information to the web server.
In its simplest form, a cookie is an identifying number.
The Forum Websites use cookies for anonymous Visitor tracking.
The Forum web server assigns each Visitor a unique numerical ID.
All requests for pages are tagged with that ID, and by analyzing log files, the Forum can determine how many unique Visitors used the Website.
The Forum does not know the identity of a person based on the numerical ID.
