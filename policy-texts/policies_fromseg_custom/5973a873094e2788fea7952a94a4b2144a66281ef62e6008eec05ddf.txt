Privacy
Conduit provides an on–demand website syndication platform that enables Publishers to create, maintain and distribute branded community toolbars.
The Conduit platform is available for free at www.conduit.com and currently serves more than 220,000 Publishers and over 100,000,000 active users from 180 countries.
As a platform provider, we are highly committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our Publishers and their users and to ensuring a satisfying experience to anyone using our services.
Conduit community toolbars are included on the industry recognized TRUSTe safe-software whitelist.
==========
The Trusted Download certification is awarded to software that demonstrates informed consumer choice, does not exhibit surreptitious activities, and is distributed in a responsible manner.
Web publishers using the Conduit platform can be assured that their branded community toolbar will be a safe and secure method for syndicating their content and applications on Internet Explorer and Firefox.
So go ahead, create your community toolbar and securely connect, engage, and bond with your users!

Our Privacy Guidelines


Commitment: We believe in a sincere, straightforward approach towards privacy issues.

Zero Tolerance: We will show zero tolerance towards Publishers who violate the privacy rights of their toolbar users and we will take legal action against any party that tries to exploit the Conduit platform for invading the privacy of others.
