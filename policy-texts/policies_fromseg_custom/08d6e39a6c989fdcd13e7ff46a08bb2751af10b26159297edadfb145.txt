Macromedia and your privacy
At Macromedia, we respect your right to privacy and we understand that visitors to macromedia.com need to be in control of their personal information.
We also want to present information on macromedia.com that is tailored to the needs of our visitors.
For this reason, we may ask you to register or to provide personal information when you visit certain areas of macromedia.com, download free software, enter a contest, or subscribe to a newsletter.
We will use the personal information you share with us to ensure the content, services, and advertising that we provide on macromedia.com (and related sites) are tailored to your current and future needs.
==========
If you provide Macromedia with your mailing address or telephone number, Macromedia and its partners may use this information to alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information, and new services.
If you provide your e-mail address, Macromedia may contact you via e-mail as well.
If you do not want Macromedia, our partners, or selected third parties to contact you, you may opt out when you provide personal information or contact Macromedia directly.
For instructions, please see the Contact Us section below.
If you choose not to register or provide personal information, you can still visit most of Macromedia's Web sites.
However, you will not have access to areas that require personal identification.
How Macromedia will protect your personal information
Macromedia will protect the personal information that you share with us.
==========
If you make a purchase on-line, we will use your credit card number only to process your payment and will not use it for marketing purposes.
If we request demographic information, we will not share information about you as an individual with any other entity.
If we do share demographic information with third parties, we will give them aggregate information only.
==========
Macromedia may carefully select third parties to send you information, by postal mail, about their products or services.
If you do not want to receive information, you may decline to receive it (or "opt out") when you provide personal information to Macromedia, or you may contact us directly.
Macromedia may occasionally present a special contest or promotion that is sponsored by another company.
To qualify for entry, we may ask you to provide personal information.
If we plan to share that information with the sponsor(s), we will provide a statement to that effect.
