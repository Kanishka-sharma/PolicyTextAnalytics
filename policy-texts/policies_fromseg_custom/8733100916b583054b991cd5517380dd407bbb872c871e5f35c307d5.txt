Office of the Privacy Advocate The Internal Revenue Service's Office of the Privacy Advocate ensures that your privacy is protected whenever you visit the IRS website.
IRS Privacy Policy We will not collect personal information about you just because you visit this Internet site.
Statistical InformationIn order to improve the website for our visitors, we automatically collect and maintain statistical information from our site's data logs that show network traffic flow and volume.
SecurityUnauthorized attempts to upload information and/or change information on this website are strictly prohibited and are subject to prosecution under the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act of 1986 and Title 18 USC Sections 1001 and 1030.
If You Send Us Information On-lineWe will only use the information you provide for the reasons you provided it.
The information will be disposed of in accordance with National Archives' records management rules.
==========
CookiesIn order to assist you or to identify you to make certain that you are the correct recipient of information that you have requested, we may use "temporary" or "session" cookies.
We do not use "permanent" cookies.
Freedom of Information Act (FOIA)The Freedom of Information Act allows access to most Federal Agency records.
Links to Other SitesThis website provides access to other web sites outside of our control.
When you link to these websites you leave our website and your communications no longer are protected by our privacy policy.
