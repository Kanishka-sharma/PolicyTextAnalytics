At Mad Mimi, protecting your privacy and the privacy of anyone receiving your email is very important.
These policies are for the purpose of making Mad Mimi safe and appropriate for all.
Policy on permission
Mad Mimi is permission-based.
That means you need permission to send to the people who receive your emails.
Mad Mimi's permission policy is part of the Terms of Use agreed to by every Mad Mimi subscriber, and conforms to Can-Spam regulations.
To send an email to someone using Mad Mimi, that recipient must be either someone who is a customer, member or subscriber of your business or organization or who has specifically asked to receive your emails by opting in or signing up in some way, orSomeone who has bought a product or service from you in the past 18 months.
