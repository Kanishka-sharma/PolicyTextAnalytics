Resale or
disclosure of information to third parties:

Operation Sports does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information
  collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.
Browser
information collected on the website:

Operation Sports analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the
  materials available on website.
Our website logs are not personally
  identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the
  individuals that actually browse the site.
Use of cookies:

We use cookies on
  Operation Sports for the sole purpose of making your visit more
  enjoyable.
They are never used for tracking or for any unethical
  reasons.
Privacy of our
email lists:

Operation Sports keeps
  email lists to deliver a weekly newsletter to those people that have requested
  it.
==========
We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on
  our list to anyone.
In addition, we configure our list server software to
  refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone.
