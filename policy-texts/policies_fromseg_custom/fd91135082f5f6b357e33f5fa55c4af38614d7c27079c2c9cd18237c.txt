Introduction
 
admetrics GmbH is an ad verification vendor.
Our goal is to increase the quality of ads and uncover invalid and fraudulent ad traffic.
We appreciate that you are aware of and care about your own personal privacy interests, and take that seriously.
This Privacy Notice outlines our policies and practices regarding its collection and use of your data.
Please recognize that information privacy is an ongoing responsibility, and so we will update this Privacy Notice as we develop new personal data practices or adopt new privacy policies.

What personal data do we process or collect?
==========
Our services enable our clients to report on ad delivery and performance by making use of web beacons and log files.
For that purpose we process IP addresses.
Why do we collect this information?
We process the IP address to find out what country it belongs to.
What do we do with this information?
After the IP address is processed it is removed from our systems.
This happens usually within a few minutes and we do not store this information in databases or any other form storage.
When and how we share information with others
 
We do not share your personal information with third parties.
