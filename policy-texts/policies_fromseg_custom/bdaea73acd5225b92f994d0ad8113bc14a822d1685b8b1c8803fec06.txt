Privacy & Security


PRIVACY
At Select Comfort, we recognize and respect our customers' privacy.
The data we collect about our customers is used solely to better meet our customer's needs through more efficient order fulfillment and improved marketing efforts.
On occasion we may share customer information with other reputable organizations whose products and services may be of interest to our customers.
We do not share customer billing information.
We adhere to all state and federal regulations regarding personal data distribution.
Select Comfort may share non-personal aggregate or summary information regarding its customers, sales, Web site traffic patterns, and related Web site information with partners or third-party vendors.
If we decide to change our Privacy and Security Policy, we will update this Policy so you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
This site contains links to other sites.
Select Comfort is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other sites.
==========
Why do we ask for an e-mail address?
Your e-mail address is collected at the time of purchase so we can easily and quickly communicate with you regarding the status of your order.
Additionally, you will receive our email newsletter.
If you do not wish to receive our complimentary newsletter, every issue gives you the opportunity to be removed from our email list.
Email newsletters update you on special offers, new products, "Comfort Club," changes to the Web site and even newsletter only specials.
==========
Do we use cookies and what are they?
Select Comfort uses a feature of your Internet Web browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer.
Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive.
Select Comfort creates and uses these cookie files to track clicks as you go through the Select Comfort Web site.
==========
This form of tracking is used to continuously improve our Web site to better meet our customers needs and make for a better site experience.
Select Comfort does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information from your computer unless you knowingly and willingly provide such information.
Select Comfort will not use cookies to store personal information, such as credit card numbers, nor can any Web site other than ours read the information we store in the cookie.
SITE SECURITY
We understand that you may be concerned about providing your personal information and credit card information online.
==========
Please be assured that we have taken precautions to ensure your shopping experience is safe and secure.
When you place an online order it is transmitted via industry standard secure protocol commonly referred to as SSL (secure socket layer).
The SSL technology that works with Netscape Navigator, Microsoft Internet Explorer, and America Online encrypts (scrambles) the information you input when placing an order, to protect against unauthorized access when the data is transmitted over the Internet.
Furthermore, all of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access.
