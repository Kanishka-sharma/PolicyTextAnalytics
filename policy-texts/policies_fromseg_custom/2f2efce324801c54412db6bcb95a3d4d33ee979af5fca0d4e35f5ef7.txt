TRUSTe Privacy Feedback
What the TRUSTe Privacy Feedback button means:
Companies whose web sites or applications display the TRUSTe Privacy Feedback button have demonstrated their commitment to privacy by utilizing TRUSTe to collect privacy feedback and complaints.
The Privacy Feedback button is not a certification seal.
All companies that display the Privacy Feedback button are solely responsible for their own privacy practices.
TRUSTe is responsible only for the privacy practices applicable to it as expressly described in the
privacy policy for the TrustArc group of companies and is not responsible for the privacy practices of any other company.
To send feedback or file a complaint, click here to fill out the online privacy feedback form.
