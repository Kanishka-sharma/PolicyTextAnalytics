California Privacy Rights & ReportAbout the California Consumer Privacy ActThe California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides that California residents may (subject to certain limitations) request to:Access the personal information we collect about themDelete the personal information we collect about themOpt out of having their personal information soldWhile we don't share personal information with third parties in exchange for money, we do disclose certain information in exchange for insights and other valuable services.
California law treats such sharing as a “sale” even if no money is exchanged.
