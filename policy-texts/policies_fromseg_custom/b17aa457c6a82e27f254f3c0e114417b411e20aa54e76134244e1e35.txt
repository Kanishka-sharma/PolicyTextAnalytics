Privacy

Conduit provides an on-demand website syndication platform that enables Publishers to create, maintain and distribute branded community toolbars.
The Conduit platform is available for free at www.conduit.com and currently serves more than 140,000 Publishers and 30,000,000 community toolbar users from 180 countries.
As a platform provider, we are highly committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our Publishers and their users and to ensuring a satisfying experience to anyone using our services.
==========
Our privacy guidelines

Commitment: We believe in a sincere, straightforward approach towards privacy issues.
We are committed to maintaining strict privacy policies, and we expect our Publishers to abide by them.
Compliance and safety: Our platform and products have been tested and certified by leading, independent security companies.
We will cooperate with any legal entity to ensure the safety and privacy of our Publishers and their users.
Zero tolerance: We will show zero tolerance towards Publishers who violate the privacy rights of their toolbar users and we will take legal action against any party that tries to exploit the Conduit platform for invading the privacy of others.
References:

Community Toolbar Privacy Policy - includes our commitment to protecting the privacy of toolbar users and the privacy standards that our Publishers must accept
Website Privacy Policy - includes our commitment to protecting the privacy of any party that visits our website
Children's Privacy policy - ensures our compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act and all other applicable laws and regulations concerning children and the Internet
Report - If you have any questions, comments or concerns regarding our Privacy Policies, or if you suspect your privacy rights have been violated in any manner, please do not hesitate to contact us via email at privacy@conduit.com, or via this contact form.
