Categories of Information That We Collect:
Personal Information
We receive and collect personally identifiable information – such as name, job title, telephone numbers, e-mail addresses, and postal addresses – from visitors to the Sites when they (i) submit inquiries or requests to us, (ii) become registered users of the Sites, (iii) sign up to receive newsletters or bulletins from us, or (iv) choose to provide information to us for some other reason.
Visitors can choose not to provide their personal information, but that decision may limit our ability to contact them and our ability to provide services to them, or respond to their inquiries.
Also, please note that if a visitor has provided us with a postal address, an e-mail address, or a telephone number, a MRP representative may contact them unless they have requested that we place them on our do-not-contact list, see “Obtaining Information From Us & Communicating With Us” below.
==========
Website Usage Information
MRP may save information on your system in the form of a small text file that is stored on your hard drive known as a “cookie” to access secure information or to better track usage of Sites.
We may use session ID cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them).
Persistent cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on the Sites.
MRP use cookies and other navigational data that is passively collected to gather information concerning the usage of the Site by that visitor, including but not limited to the visitor’s Internet domain and/or IP address, information concerning the visitor’s browser, and other access-related information.
We also may assemble aggregate information (information obtained by compiling information from many different visitors) as to which pages a visitor accesses on the Site.
==========
This information is used to analyze the Sites’ utility, and to enhance the areas and functionality within the Site that appear to be of interest to various users.
We use this information in connection with developing, delivering and improving our services for our Clients and Partners.
The information collected is not analyzed in a way that would reveal the identity of the individual browsing the Sites.
Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but visitors can manage cookies by following directions provided in their Internet browser’s “help” file.
If a visitor rejects cookies, they may still use the Sites, but their ability to use some Sites areas’, such as contests or surveys, will be limited.
==========
Client Information
We collect information (such as contact, billing, account, and business data) under the direction of our Clients and this data is used to offer products, fulfill orders, respond to customer service request and administer customer accounts.
The use of information collected through our Client service is limited to the purpose of providing that Client service.
Visitors who are a customer of one of our Clients and would no longer like to be contacted by them should contact the Client directly.
Business information
MRP collect information on businesses and business professionals from company websites, vendors, public records and third party suppliers including company statistics such as employee numbers, geographic locations and contact information.
Supplemental Information
We may obtain personally identifiable and other information about visitors from our subsidiary companies, business partners, vendors or Clients and add it to information that they have previously provided to us in order to serve them better.
From time to time, we may compare our contact lists to lists received from our subsidiary companies, third party service providers or Clients to try to avoid sending visitors unwanted, unnecessary or duplicative messages.
==========
We may also utilize and incorporate information from other sources to update and supplement information concerning visitors.
All such combined, updated, and supplemented information will be handled consistent with this Policy.
Use of Collected Information:
Any personally identifiable information provided will be used by us or our service providers or vendors in connection with providing services for and communicating with visitors including processing any requests or inquiries that they initiate.
Our service providers or vendors may not use visitor information for their own marketing purposes.
The use of information collected through a Site operated for a Client is limited to the purpose of providing the services for the Client.
MRP will display relevant ads tailored to visitors based on (i) visits to our Sites (ii) key words that you search for and (iii) the content topics of web pages you visit.
==========
We will share the data needed to do this with third parties for this purpose, and for aggregate market analysis purposes.
Information provided by visitors will not be sold, transferred, or traded to any third parties for the marketing of third parties’ goods or services without the visitor’s consent.
If we decide to offer visitors the opportunity to “opt-in” for data sharing with third parties, we will only share their data consistent with that visitor’s opt-in preferences and visitors may subsequently “opt-out” by seeing “Obtaining Information From Us & Communicating With Us” below.
