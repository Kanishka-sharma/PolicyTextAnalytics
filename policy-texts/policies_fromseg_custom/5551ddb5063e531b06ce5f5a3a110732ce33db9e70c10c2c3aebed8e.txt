Cookie Audit
EU Cookie Audit
The EU Cookie Directive requires companies that use tracking technologies on their websites to obtain prior informed consent before placing these technologies on customers' devices.
An important first step is to fully understand what trackers are on your website.
TRUSTe's Website Tracker Audit allows you to uncover, analyze and monitor first and third party trackers so that you can ensure that you are accurate in your disclosures to customers.

US-EU Safe Harbor
For US companies exporting EU citizens data to the US, the US Department of Commerce developed the US-EU Safe Harbor Framework help maintain privacy standards while enabling business across borders.
The TRUSTe EU Safe Harbor Program verifies your compliance with the US-EU Safe Harbor Framework, provides 3rd party dispute resolution, and helps your organization prepare for self-certification with the U.S. Department of Commerce.
