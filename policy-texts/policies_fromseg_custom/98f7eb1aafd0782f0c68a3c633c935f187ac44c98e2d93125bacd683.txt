Do we use cookies?
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns.
SquareBUX.com uses cookies to help identify and track visitors.
SquareBUX.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using our website, with the drawback that certain features of SquareBUX.com may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
