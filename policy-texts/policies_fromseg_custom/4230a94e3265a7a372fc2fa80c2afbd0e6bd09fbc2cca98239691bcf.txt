Information Collection 
Media.net collects and stores personally identifiable information when you provide it to us through our Website - http://www.media.net or http://contextualads.yahoo.net.
"Personally identifiable Information" means any information obtained through your use of our Website which might reasonably be used to specifically identify you; which may include your name, title, company, address, email and phone number and any other personal information you choose to provide while using our Website or our ad services.
Our ad serving technology does not collect any personally identifiable information about users during the ad delivery process.
We may also collect non-personally identifiable information regarding your and other users' behavior and usage patterns on advertisements and on websites operated by our publisher partners to better customize the type of advertisements you and other users see on various sites.
Specifically, we collect the following information:

Log Information.
This is technical information from a user's browser that is automatically collected when users visit our website or Partner Sites managed by our publisher partners.
This information includes an IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, the content of any undeleted cookies that the browser previously accepted from us (see "Cookies" below), and the referring website address.
Cookies.
A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user's computer that tracks non-personal information about the user.
==========
When using our website or visiting our Partner Sites, we may assign a user's computer one or more cookies which enable us to improve the quality of our service.
Cookies allow us to automatically collect information about activity on our website and Partner Sites, the links or advertisements you click, or the other actions conduct by users.
The cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.
Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to disable cookies.
If you choose to disable cookies, you will still be able to access our website, but you may not be able to use all of the interactive features we offer.
