Cookies
We or our service providers may place "cookies" (a "cookie" is a small files placed on the hard drive of your computer) and "web beacons" (a "web beacon" is an electronic image, called a single-pixel or clear GIF placed in the web page code) on your computer when you visit portions of Gumtree.
Most cookies are "session cookies," meaning that they are automatically deleted from your hard drive at the end of a session, for example, when you close your browser.
We may use permanent or session cookies to help analyse our web page flow; customise our services, content and advertising; measure promotional effectiveness, and promote trust and safety.
==========
You can block our cookies by changing the settings on your browser.
Find out more about Accepting and Deleting Cookies, but doing so may prevent us from delivering certain services to you.
You may encounter cookies from third parties on certain pages of the site that we do not control.
==========
We use third-party ad-serving technologies, to serve ads, and they may place cookies on your computer.
We do not allow these third parties to collect any personal information through the use of cookies in ads.
