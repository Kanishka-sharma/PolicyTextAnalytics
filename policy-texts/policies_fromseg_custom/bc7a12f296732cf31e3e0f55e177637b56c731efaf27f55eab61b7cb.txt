DynAdmic provides advertising services (our “Services”) to a variety of companies.
Our services are primarily focused on helping advertisers to serve relevant ads to the right audience at the right time.
DynAdmic is offering a new targeting concept based on a video content recognition technology that helps identify viewers' interests to serve the right ad to the right person.
(Demo of concept: https://www.dynadmic.com/howitworks.)
We use our never-before-seen contextual targeting solution to sell tailored and highly focused branding campaigns to the world's largest brands and agencies.
==========
Our clients include Emirates, Red Bull, Kellogg's, Audi, etc.
This Privacy Policy describes how we handle the information we collect and receive about users.
Information we receive and collect
We collect your information when you and others are using browsers and devices while visiting or using websites, apps, and advertisements that use our Services.
We collect some of this information using cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies.
The information we collect includes: device type, operating system, unique identifiers, IP address, location, browser type & language, and header information such as URLs and date/time stamps.
We do not collect personally identifiable information, such as your name or email address.
We use all of the information we gather to improve and provide our advertising, measurement, and reporting Services.
Depending on how you access and use our Services, we may receive information related to:
                        -	Your interactions with the websites, apps, and other services you use; the content you view; and your clicks or engagements on ads
                        -	How you access those websites, apps, and other services
                        -	Your browser or operating system
                        -	Your IP address and the website you visited before visiting our Services
                        -	The type of device you are using (i.e. iPhone, tablet, Dell desktop)
                        -	Your location based on your IP address
                        -	Your location based on the GPS coordinates of your mobile/tablet device
                        -	The Identifier for Advertisers (IFA or IDFA) of your mobile/tablet device
                        -	Your DynAdmic user ID, an identifier randomly generated by DynAdmic stored as a cookie on your browser

                                                    We also collect information from third parties, such as our customers and partners, which includes advertisers, marketing partners, and service providers related to their use and support of our Services.
The information can include demographics, purchase information, interests, and any advertising ID or cookie which helps us determine which ads are shown on your browser or device and helps us measure the effectiveness of the ads.
The collection and use of that data is governed by the privacy policy and legal terms of the data collector and the website using the data; it is not governed by DynAdmic.

Use of the information we have
We use the information we have to find and match appropriate audiences with appropriate advertisements and to serve customized ads to your browser or device.
To do this, we may use information we have about your activities over time and across different websites, apps, browsers, and devices.
We may also decide to show an ad based on the website or app you are using, the video content you are watching, things you may be interested in, or requests from our advertising partners, who decide to show ads to a particular audience, sometimes using information they have on file.
We also use the information we have to optimize ad delivery, such as managing the frequency and the order in which the ads are shown.
To be more efficient, we can use the information we collected to associate the browsers and devices you use so we can provide better and more consistent experiences across browsers and devices, as well as improve our Services.
We also use the information we have for the following purposes:
                        -	Limiting the number of times you see the same ad
                        -	Measure the effectiveness of the ads we serve
                        -	Provide more effective measurements and reporting Services.

How to exercise your rights on your data
According to The Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016, you have the following rights:

                        -	Right of Access:
                        You have the right to obtain confirmation from us as to whether or not personal data concerning you is processed.
Where that is the case, you have the right to request and get access to that personal data.
-	Right to Rectification:
                        You have the right to obtain the rectification of inaccurate personal data from us, and you have the right to provide additional personal data to complete any incomplete personal data.
-	Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”):
                        You have the right to obtain from us the erasure of your personal data.
-	Right to Restriction of Processing:
                        You have the right to obtain restriction of processing from us, which is applicable for a certain period and/or for certain situations.
-	Right to Data Portability:
                            You have the right to receive your personal data from us in a structured format, and you have the right to allow transmit such personal data to another controller.
You can exercise your rights by sending us a message using the form in the “How to contact us?”
section below.
If, after contacting us, you consider that your rights are not respected, you can contact The French Data Protection Authority (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés) (CNIL) at https://www.cnil.fr/en/contact-cnil/.

Transparency & Consent Framework
DynAdmic is referenced on the list of vendors adhering to the IAB Transparency & Consent Framework (https://vendorlist.consensu.org/vendorlist.json).
The IAB Transparency & Consent Framework has a simple objective to help all parties in the digital advertising chain ensure that they comply with the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation and ePrivacy Directive when processing personal data or accessing and/or storing information on a user’s device.
This includes cookies, advertising identifiers, device identifiers, and other tracking technologies.
