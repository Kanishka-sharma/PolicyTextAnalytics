I. Information Which The Post Collects or Obtains
The Post collects information in several ways by your use of this Site.
The Post may ask you to voluntarily provide The Post or its Affiliates with personal information on this Site, or The Post's Affiliates may ask you to voluntarily provide personal information on their web sites and may share such personal information with us.
For example, The Post may collect personal information from you when you register to use this Site, when you participate in certain forums, bulletin boards or other interactive areas of this Site or if you otherwise communicate with The Post or its Affiliates through this Site, whether by email or otherwise.
During these instances, you may be asked to provide certain personal information, such as your name, postal address, email address, telephone number, credit card information, user name and password, certain demographic information, such as age, gender or marital status, or information about your children from the ages of 14 through 17.
Whenever The Post collects information about you, The Post will make an effort to link to this Privacy Policy, and will treat information The Post receives from The Post's Affiliates in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
==========
Anyone under the age of 14 may not use this Site or the services available on or through this Site and should not provide personally identifiable information through this Site.
The Post will not knowingly request personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 14 without first obtaining verifiable parental consent.
Like many other commercial web sites, The Post utilizes a standard technology called a "cookie" to automatically collect information about how this Site is used and to enhance your visit upon your return to this Site.
Cookies were designed to help a web site recognize a user's browser as a previous visitor and thus save and remember any preferences that may have been set while the user was browsing the web site.
The Post's cookies may collect your domain name and track your selections through this Site.
The Post and its Affiliates may use cookies to alert you to new areas of this Site that The Post thinks might be of interest to you when you return to this Site, to deliver targeted advertising based on your behavior or demographics, to record user-specific information on what pages you access, to record session information, such as items you purchase or sell or place in a shopping cart, and to record other information about your use of this Site.
You can set your browser to refuse to accept cookies, but if you do The Post will not be able to customize this Site's features according to your preferences.
