Privacy
This statement discloses the privacy practices for the Official Site of the Roland Garros 2014.
The French Tennis Federation intends to give you as much control as possible over your personal information.
==========
In general, you can view this site without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself.
There are times, however, when we may need information from you, such as your name and address.
==========
It is our intent to let you know before we collect personal information from you on the Internet.
If you choose to give us personal information via the Internet that we may need -- to correspond with you, process an order or provide you with a subscription, for example -- it is our intent to let you know how we will use such information.
==========
If you tell us that you do not wish to have this information used as a basis for further contact with you, we will respect your wishes.
If you tell us that you do not wish to have this information distributed to others, or if you tell us that you do not wish to receive additional information from us or our partners, we will respect your wishes.
==========
We do keep track of the domains from which users visit us.
We analyze this data for trends and statistics, and then we discard it.
==========
Business Relationships
The Official Site of the Roland Garros 2014 contains links to other Web Sites.
The French Tennis Federation is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web Sites.
==========
Cookies
Cookies are small data files that your Internet browser receives from our Site and stores on your hard drive.
The use of "cookies" makes the management of our Site easier, because they allow to recognize an Internet user when he/she comes back on the Site, and/or carry statistical studies (pages viewed, date and time of visit) and to measure the audience share, so as to provide a better service, as personalized as possible.
==========
The cookie keeps in memory only technical data that are related to the connection, except any other personal information concerning you.
It cannot export personal data from your computer.
==========
To delete cookies or oppose to their use, you can refer to the software notices indicating you how to follow the technical procedure which enables to refuse and/or to delete cookies.
We inform you that refusing cookies may restrain your access to all or part of the Site.
