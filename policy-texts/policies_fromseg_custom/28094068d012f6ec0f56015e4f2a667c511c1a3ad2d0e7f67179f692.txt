Tax deductions can recover stock losses
By Attorney Brian Isaacson
After the market decline in 2000, 2001 and 2002, many taxpayers have incurred investment losses for worthless securities.
Although they may not be able to recover their investment, they may be entitled to a tax deduction.

Random Term From The Legal Dictionary
Embargo
This is an act of international military aggression where an order is made prohibiting ships or goods from leaving a certain port, city or territory and may be enforced by military threat of destroying any vehicle that attempts to break it or by trade penalties.
The word has also come to refer to a legal prohibition of trade with a certain nation or a prohibition towards the use of goods or services produced by or within a certain nation.
