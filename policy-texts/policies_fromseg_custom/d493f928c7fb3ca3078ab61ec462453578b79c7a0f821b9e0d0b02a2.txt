Q: What are cookies, and do you use them?
A: According to the Merriam Webster dictionary, a cookie is "a small
 flat or slightly raised cake."
It can also be a small file that is
 stored on a user's computer by a Web server in order to keep
 track of user patterns and preferences.
WhitePages.com makes limited
 use of cookies in order to provide personalized content and protect
 confidential user information.
However, in no event do we obtain personally
 identifiable information from our use of cookies.
You may choose not to accept
 cookies by changing the preferences on your browser.
However, your experience
 will be less personalized and, in addition, certain services may not
 be available for your use.
