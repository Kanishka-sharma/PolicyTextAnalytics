“Anonymous Data” means data that is not associated with or linked to your Personal Data; Anonymous Data does not permit the identification of individual persons.

==========
“Cookies” are small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer’s hard drive while you are viewing a web site.
We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on our website.
Persistent Cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions.
If you choose to disable Cookies, some areas of our website may not work properly.
