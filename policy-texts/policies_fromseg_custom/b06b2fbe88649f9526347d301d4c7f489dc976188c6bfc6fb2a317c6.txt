Part I. General Information
Our commitment to privacy
Sift Science, Inc. (“Sift Science”, “we” or “us”) helps online businesses (our “Customers”) detect and address fraud and other malicious behavior on their digital properties.
In doing so, we collect information about how Internet users (“Users”) interact with our Customers’ digital properties such as their websites and mobile applications.
We keep privacy firmly in mind when developing, designing and operating our cloud-based, machine-learning platform that is used to predict user intent and prevent fraudulent activity in real time (“the Sift Service”).
This Website Privacy Policy provides an explanation of our online information practices as well as the decisions you may make regarding how your information is collected and used on our website, siftscience.com, and its sub-domains (the "Website").
Please note that this Website Privacy Policy does not describe our collection and use of information when we provide the Sift Service to our Customers.
For an explanation of how we collect and use information when we perform the Sift Service, please see our Service Privacy Policy.

What We Collect and How We Use It
Information we collect through our Website
We may collect the following types of information about you on our Website.
Information that you provide to us when you use the Website
We may collect personal information about you (e.g. full name, email address, phone number) so we can fulfill your request for the Sift Service or so that a member of our sales team can provide you with information you have requested about the Sift Service.
You may also provide information about your employer, such as company name, URL, fraud-related information, and company size.
==========
We may collect credit card information and billing contact information where necessary for billing purposes.
We may also use the information you provide to us for our marketing purposes if this is in accordance with your marketing preferences.
However, you will always be able to opt-out of our marketing at any time (see the "Unsubscribe from Our Mailing List" section below).
==========
Information that we collect automatically when you use the Website
When you visit our Website, Sift Science, like most website owners, collects certain information related to your device, such as your device's IP address, referring website, what pages your device visited, and the time that your device visited our Website.
We (including our vendors) may use cookies, pixel tags, web beacons, embedded web links and similar technologies.
You can choose to reject certain collection technologies (such as cookies) but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our features.
We will use this information:
administer our Website and for internal operations, including troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, research, statistical and survey purposes;
to understand how our Website is used and to improve our Website to ensure that content is presented in the most effective manner for you and your computer;
to provide advertising to you on third party sites, based on browsing activities on the Website; and,
as part of our efforts to keep our Website safe and secure.
Sift Science does not collect information about your online activities across different web sites, over time.
Accordingly, Sift Science does not currently respond to “do not track” browser requests.

Sharing and disclosure of information to third parties
We may share and disclose information about you in the following circumstances:
Vendors, consultants and other service providers: We may share your information with third party vendors, consultants and other service providers who are working on our behalf and with whom the sharing of your information is necessary to undertake that work, e.g. to process billing, to provide customer support, and to deliver online and offline marketing communications that we think will interest you.
Compliance with laws: We may disclose your information to a third party where we are legally required to do so in order to comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or government request.
Vital interests and legal rights: We may disclose information about you if we believe it necessary to protect the vital interests or legal rights of Sift Science, our Customers or the rights or property of others.
==========
Corporate Affiliates and Transactions: We may provide your information to our affiliates (meaning any subsidiary, parent company or company under common control with Sift).
Our affiliates will use your information only as described in this Website Privacy Policy.
Additionally, if Sift Science is involved in a merger, acquisition or sale of all or portion of its assets, your information may be shared or transferred as part of that transaction, as permitted by law.

International Transfer and Security
Processing of information in the U.S.
While visitors may access the Website from outside of the United States, information collected via the Website is transferred to and hosted on our servers in the United States.
We will protect your information in accordance with this Website Privacy Policy wherever it is processed.
Security safeguards
We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect any personal information we process about visitors to our Website against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
==========
However, please note that no Internet transmission can ever be guaranteed 100% secure, and so we encourage you to take care when disclosing personal information online and to use readily available tools, such as Internet firewalls, secure e-mail and similar technologies to protect yourself online.
