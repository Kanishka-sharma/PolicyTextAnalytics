Data protection declaration

                With this data protection declaration we would like to inform you about how we process personal data.
We are aware of the importance of the processing of personal data for the user and accordingly observe all relevant legal requirements.
The protection of your privacy is of the utmost importance to us.
==========
Therefore, it goes without saying that we comply with the legal provisions on data protection.
Group-oriented data protection

                To what extent and in what form we process and store personal data depends on the respective group of persons.
==========
We differentiate as follows: Publishers, merchants and users.
Publisher are website operators who use our service by implementing the digidip code to activate the digidip service on their website.
The control and ongoing use of the digidip account is carried out via a web-based portal, which is available via login in the browser.
Merchants are advertisers (or agencies or networks who represent merchants) who use the digidip technology to network with publishers who use our service.
If desired, the control and use of the digidip merchant account takes place via a web-based portal, which is available via a login in the browser.
Under partners we combine both groups, merchants and publishers.
Users are those Internet users who use the Publishers' websites.

Cookies

                Cookies are small files that your browser places on your device in a designated location directory.
These cookies can be used, for example, to determine whether you have visited a website or not have visited us before.
Most browsers automatically accept cookies.
However, you can set your browser so that no cookies are stored or explicit consent is required before a cookie is saved.
You can also delete cookies already set at any time.
Please note that disabling Cookies may lead to restrictions on the use of our website.
We mainly use so-called session cookies, which support the use of our website.
These cookies are automatically deleted after a certain period of inactivity or when the browser is closed.
