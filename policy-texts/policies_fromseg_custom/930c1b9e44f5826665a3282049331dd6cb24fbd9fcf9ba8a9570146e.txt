How we collect Information
The Service uses cookies, beacons, mobile SDKs and pixels to collect Information associated with a browser or mobile device.
Adsolutions doesn’t participate in the exchange of IDs with partners.
Cookies are small text files stored in a web browser by a website or ad server.
By saving information in a cookie, websites and servers can remember preferences or recognize web browsers from one visit to another or from one website to another.
Beacons, pixels, tagging scripts, and mobile SDKs are elements allowing communication between a web browser and a server.
==========
A beacon or pixel is a small transparent image that is placed on a web page.
A tagging script is a small piece of website code that is run by the web browser.
A mobile SDK is a piece of computer code that runs in mobile applications.

Our Use of Cookies
Adsolutions uses cookies for delivery purposes.
When we refer to the term “cookies”, we mean cookies and other identifiers, such as mobile advertising identifiers.
Generally, cookies enable our clients to target ads to users, to evaluate how well their ads work, and to see users’ opt-out choices.
The purposes for which we use cookies are as follows:

The use of cookies allows us to store data about the delivery of ads for reporting purposes, such as which ad was shown, the frequency of an ad, and the last time an ad has been delivered to a particular browser.
The use of cookies enables our clients to track user responses (clicks and conversions) to ads.
The use of cookies to detect ad-fraud.

Data Retention
The Cookies Adsolutions sets in browsers expire with a maximum of 356 days after the last interaction.
Mobile Advertising IDs are technical IDs in mobile application environments.
Mobile Advertising IDs are resettable by the user; however Adsolutions does not have control of a Mobile Advertising ID.
Adsolutions will see the same Mobile Advertising ID as long as it is not changed by the device user or updated by the device.
Adsolutions does not keep any Cookie-based Information longer than 12 month.
In other words, any data that can be related to your internet devices are deleted after maximum 12 months.

Header Bidding by Adsolutions Platform  
Adsolutions customer base consists of publisher and sales houses that use our software to connect to buyers of advertising space on their websites.
The websites of most of our clients are accessible free of charge.
They use advertising revenue to cover their cost, and in most cases, to continue to provide their services publicly.
==========
Through our platform publishers are able to connect to several buyers of advertising in real-time.
Our platform basically functions as a regular auction house.
Information about the visitors and websites passes through our platform to the buyers of advertising.
These buyers bid on the advertising space and the highest bidder wins.
==========
The information of visitors was collected by publishers and advertisers.
Publishers and advertisers use cookies to store this information.
These cookies can pass through our platform.
Technically our platform can make use of functional cookies, necessary to run an auction, and non-functional, user identifiable, cookies.
For making use of non-functional cookies, we need your approval.
When you visit a website from a company that makes use of our platform the website will ask your permission (opt-in) to do this.
The one thing we log and store which is identifiable as personal data is a unique user ID.
==========
Which is basically a number that’s connected to your browser.
We don’t have access to any other information which could be connected to this number.
It is possible at all times to change your mind and opt-out.
