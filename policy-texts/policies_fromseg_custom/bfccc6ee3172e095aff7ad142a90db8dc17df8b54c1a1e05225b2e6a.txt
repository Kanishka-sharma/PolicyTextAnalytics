Through Our Services

Duration Media LLC does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) (name, address, phone number or email address).
We don’t store or request anything of the PII nature.
We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or email address.
Our clients/partners also do not share this information with us, and we require that they either not collect PII, or, that they not include PII with anonymous data they may share with us.
==========
At times you may choose to voluntarily provide such information to an advertiser through their website or advertisement.
When you provide information to an advertiser, Duration Media is not involved in any way in this exchange of information and you should read the privacy policy of the advertiser to understand how your information will be used.
Duration Media does accumulate certain non-personally identifiable information through the use of cookies and pixels when you come upon our clients’ advertisements.
This anonymous data might include web browser type (e.g. Chrome), computer operating system (e.g. Mac OS), geographic location as interpreted from your IP address, websites visited, and other information that does not specifically identify you.
We utilize this non-personally identifiable information collected from a computer to serve targeted ads to that computer based upon its browsing history.
We may share this non-personally identifiable information with third party companies to pair it against other data sets.
If we ever inadvertently collect PII through our service, we will not knowingly use such PII in connection with our advertising targeting services.
