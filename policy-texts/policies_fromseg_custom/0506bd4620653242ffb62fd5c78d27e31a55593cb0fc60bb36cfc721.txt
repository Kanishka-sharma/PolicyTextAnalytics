Invite a Friend
If a user decides to use our Invitation option to invite a friend to our site, we ask them for the friend's e-mail address.
Badoo will automatically send the friend an e-mail inviting them to join the site.
Badoo stores this e-mail address for the purpose of automatically associating the friend with the user sending the invitation, and also to send reminders of the invitation.
Badoo does not sell these e-mail addresses or use them to send any other communication besides invitations and invitation reminders.
The friend may contact Badoo to request the removal of this information from our database.
Uploading your Contacts
If a user decides to upload their contacts, they accept that by doing so, an e-mail invitation will be sent on their behalf, to the e-mail addresses of the contacts that are being uploaded and that from time to time, a reminder e-mail could be sent to that contact.
Badoo stores the name and e-mail address of the contacts for the purpose of automatically associating the friend with the user sending the invitation and to send reminders of the invitation on the user's behalf.
Badoo does not sell these e-mail addresses or use them to send any other communication besides invitations and invitation reminders.
