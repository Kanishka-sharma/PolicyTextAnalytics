Cookies
TigerDirect, its service providers or other third parties may set cookies for your web browser when you visit our website, use our products or visit a website where TigerDirect provides ads, content or analytics.
Use of cookies by TigerDirect
Upon visiting our Site, we will assign you a unique ID in the form of a "cookie".
A cookie is a piece of information that is sent to your browser by a website that help websites such as our Site to remember information about your visit and save your preferred settings.
Cookies are also used for such purposes as facilitating and personalizing your shopping and ordering experience, keeping track of your shopping basket during your visit, helping diagnose problems with our server and administering our Site.
In our case, we may use the information collected through cookies for marketing purposes to inform you of special offers, to better understand your needs, or to answer your questions.
==========
There are two types of cookies: first party cookies which are created by the site you visit and third party cookies which are created by other sites who own some of the content on the site you are visiting.
You retain the ability at any time at your discretion to manage and control how and whether cookies will be accepted by your web browser for both types of cookies as best suits your needs.
Some browsers even allow you to manage cookies on a site-by-site basis.
You may also limit or delete cookies by reviewing your cookie settings and ad settings.
==========
For example, if you use a Google Chrome browser, the Tools menu contains an option to Clear Browsing Data.
You can use this option to delete cookies and other site and plug-in data, including data stored on your device by the Adobe Flash Player (commonly known as Flash cookies).
We recommend, however, that you allow cookies from our site as they enable you to utilize our features and services and limiting or deleting cookies will mean you will be signed out of our Site, lose any of your saved preferences on our Site, and be prevented from adding items to your shopping cart and proceeding to Checkout.
