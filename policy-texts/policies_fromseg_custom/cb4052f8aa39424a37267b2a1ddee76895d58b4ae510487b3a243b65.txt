Privacy Policy
Privacy
mbr targeting respects your privacy.
Our technology and the manner in which we use it meets or exceeds all industry standards and legal requirements regarding online privacy protecion.
This privacy policy applies to all mbr targeting web sites, products and services worldwide that display or link to this notice.
==========
The purpose of this privacy policy is to provide you with comprehensive information relating to the data we collect and how it is used, and inform you of your choices with respect to the collection and use of data for online behavioral advertising.
Our purpose in collecting such data is to help us provide you with better service, such as more relevant notifications about special offers and promotions.
==========
We will not willfully disclose any individually identifiable information about you to any third party without first receiving your permission, as explained in this privacy statement.
Furthermore, we do not collect any personal information except in the case that an individual specifically and intentionally provides such information, and we specifically require the use of said information for our services.
Upon receiving such a request for information, we provide an explanation regarding how this information may be used as well as an opportunity to request that the information not be used for those purposes.
In addition, we give you an opportunity to opt out of future communications from us.
==========
Cookies and anonymous data ("non-PII")
In the same way as most sites, mbr targeting uses cookies for account management purposes.
Cookies can be defined as data files stored on your computer that identify you as a unique user.
==========
Cookies enable us to track user trends, preferences and patterns to improve our understanding of our user base and thus improve the quality of our service.
Most browsers allow users to refuse the acceptance of cookies or to be notified before a cookie is accepted.
It is possible to visit our site without the use of cookies.
==========
Personally Identifiable Information ("PII")
We only collect PII (such as your name, email address, phone number) when you personally provide it to us through our contact form with the intention of enabling direct communication between you and us.
Upon receiving this information, we use it to respond to your inquiry or comment.
We then store said information for a reasonable time in order to complete correspondence.
We never acquire PII through ads or cookies nor do we associate cookies to PII that might have been received from you for the purposes of business communication.
==========
We will never share your PII with third parties unless obligated by the national law of the Federal Republic of Germany or international governance.
With Whom Do We Share Information?
mbr targeting may share non-personal and non-personally identifiable information about our users with advertisers and other third parties.
However, we only divulge aggregate information.
==========
We will not share personally identifiable information with any third party without your explicit consent.
Consent and Changes to this Privacy Statement
By using our site, you agree to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this privacy statement and consent to our Terms of Service.
