We may also run certain promotions or sweepstakes that

						information from you about your shopping and product preferences, though you are 
						not required to give that information.
==========
You do not have to provide us with any Personally Identifiable Information to 
						visit our Website.
In fact, you may browse most of the pages on our Website 
						without giving us any Personally Identifiable Information.
If you choose to 
						withhold requested information, however, you may not be able to use some 
						features of our Website.
We use the Personally Identifiable Information we collect on our Website for 
						the explicit purpose for which it is requested.
If you identify yourself by

						Identifiable Information to respond to your questions or comments.
We also may 
						use your Personally Identifiable Information to communicate with you about your

Does Dollar General disclose my Personally Identifiable Information to third parties?
Dollar General uses your Personally Identifiable Information only to serve you better.
==========
Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy, on our Website or as otherwise disclosed at 
						the time we request your Personally Identifiable Information, we will not share any of 
						your Personally Identifiable Information with any third party without your permission.
We also will not rent or sell your Personally Identifiable Information to any third party.
We limit access to the Personally Identifiable Information that we collect to those

We require all of our employees

We reserve the right to transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to a third 
						party in the event of a sale, merger or other transfer of all or substantially all of

Otherwise, we will not disclose any of your Personally Identifiable Information unless we have 
						your express permission, we are required by legal subpoena to do so, we believe in good faith 
						that the disclosure is otherwise required by law, or to enforce our Terms of Use.

We use such 
						non-personally identifiable information to help us understand who uses our
