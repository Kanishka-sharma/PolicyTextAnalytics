Mathway and your privacy
At Mathway we respect your right to privacy and we understand that, as a visitor to Mathway.com, you prefer to control your own personal information and preferences.
For this reason, we may ask you to provide information when you visit certain areas of Mathway.com, or request information.
When you provide Mathway with your name (or alias), e-mail address, mailing address, or telephone number, and you do not 'opt out,' Mathway may use the provided information to alert you to product upgrades, special offers, product releases, and other Mathway information.
Mathway does not offer or allow the selling of any user-provided information to third parties.
Mathway respects the rights users give us when opting to receive e-mail communications and enforces internal policies to preserve those rights.
It is our objective to retain the long-term ability to continue to communicate with our users.
==========
If you do not want Mathway to contact you, you may 'opt out' of this preference at any time, whether you're online or contacting Mathway directly.
As part of your preference flexibility, you'll always have the option to opt back in and have Mathway contact you based on your previous or new preferences.
Mathway uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website.
==========
These companies may use information (NOT including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
How Mathway will protect your personal information
Mathway will always protect the personal information that you share with us.
Mathway stores information internally in a controlled, secure environment.
If Mathway requests general demographic information, this information is limited to reporting purposes only; members remain anonymous.
Phone numbers, e-mail addresses, and postal addresses are never shared for demographic reports with outside entities.
==========
Though we make every effort to preserve user privacy, we may need to disclose personal information when required by law wherein we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our website.
Links
Mathway websites may contain links to other sites.
Mathway assumes no responsibility for the content or the privacy policies and practices of such websites.
Mathway encourages you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites; their privacy practices may differ from ours.
