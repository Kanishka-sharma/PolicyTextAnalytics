Election protests: Seven reported killed as Ahmadiejad leaves Tehran
Iranian state television said on Tuesday seven people had been killed near the site of a rally in Tehran while Ahmadinejad defied the mass protests by visiting Russia for a summit.
It was not specified if the dead were opposition supporters or others.
Demonstrators on the streets of Tehran say that the election was rigged to secure victory for the hardline Ahmadinejad.
Fatal kidnappings: Three German women captives killed in Yemen
Three German women have been found dead in north Yemen, a government and a tribal source told Reuters on Monday, a rare killing that comes as separatist and militant tensions intensify.
Dubai-based security analyst Fares bin Houzam said it was possible al Qaeda was behind the deaths.

Tbilisi: Police beat protesters in Georgian capital
Tensions are running high in the former Soviet republic, after more than two months of opposition protests and roadblocks demanding Saakashvili quit over his record on democracy and last year's disastrous war with Russia.

Protests: Ahmadinejad postpones Russia visit after election
Iranian President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad has faced demonstrations and clashes in the streets since Saturday, when officials announced his landslide victory in the election on Friday against a moderate opponent.
He has delayed attending a regional security summit in Russia on Monday after protests against his contested re-election.
