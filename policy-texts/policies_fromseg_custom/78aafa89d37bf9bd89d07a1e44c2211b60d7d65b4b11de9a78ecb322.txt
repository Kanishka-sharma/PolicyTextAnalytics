==========
What is the scope of this Privacy Policy?

2.
==========
What personal information is collected from visitors to our Website?
“Personally Identifiable Information” is information we collect on our Website 
						that can be used to identify or contact you, including, but not limited to, 
						your name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.
We only 
						collect Personally Identifiable Information you voluntarily choose to provide 
						to us.
For example, we will ask you to provide Personally Identifiable 
						Information when you contact our Customer Service so that we may respond to 
						your question or comment when appropriate.
==========
From time to time, we may supplement 
						Personally Identifiable Information collected on our Website with information 
						collected by third parties.
You do not have to provide us with any Personally Identifiable Information to 
						visit our Website.
If you choose to withhold requested information, however, 
						you may not be able to use some features of our Website.
==========
We will use your Personally Identifiable Information only for the purposes 
						described in this Privacy Policy or as otherwise disclosed at the time we 
						request such information from you.
We will not use your Personally Identifiable 
						Information for any other purposes without your permission.
We use the Personally Identifiable Information we collect on our Website for 
						the explicit purpose for which it is requested.
If you identify yourself by 
						sending us an e-mail with questions or comments, we may use your Personally 
						Identifiable Information to respond to your questions or comments.
We also may 
						use your Personally Identifiable Information to communicate with you about your 
						use of our Website.
3.
==========
Does Dollar General use “cookies”?
Our Website does not use “cookies,” which are small pieces of information that 
						are stored by the browser on your computer’s hard drive that allow you to be 
						recognized when you return to a website.
Our Website may collect IP addresses from you which will be used only to 
						compile statistical data relating to the use of our Website.
We use such 
						non-personally identifiable information to help us understand who uses our 
						Website and to improve and market our Website
						

						4.
==========
Does Dollar General collect personal information on children?
Dollar General is committed to protecting the privacy of children.
Our Website 
						is not designed for or directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not 
						knowingly collect personal information about children under the age of 13.
If 
						we become aware that a child under the age of 13 has provided personal 
						information to us, we will delete the information from our records immediately.
5.
==========
Does Dollar General disclose my personal information?
Dollar General uses your Personally Identifiable Information only to serve you 
						better.
We do not use that information to contact you for advertising or 
						promotional purposes.
==========
We also do not rent or sell any personal information 
						collected through our Website to any third parties.
We will not otherwise disclose any of your Personally Identifiable Information 
						unless we have your express permission, we are required by legal subpoena to do 
						so, we believe in good faith that the disclosure is otherwise required by law, 
						or to enforce our Terms of Use.
In addition, we limit access to the Personally Identifiable Information that we 
						collect to those within our organization with a business need to know.
We 
						require all of our employees to maintain the confidentiality of confidential 
						information entrusted to them unless disclosure is duly authorized or is 
						required by law.
We may disclose your Personally Identifiable Information to our affiliates, 
						provided that such affiliates will be obligated to maintain the privacy of such 
						information and not to disclose such information to other entities.
Our Website does contain links to other websites.
==========
We attempt to make it obvious 
						to you when you leave our Website by opening a new browser.
Please be aware 
						that we do not control and are not responsible for the content and privacy 
						practices of such websites.
==========
We reserve the right to transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to a 
						third party in the event of a sale, merger or other transfer of all or 
						substantially all of the assets of Dollar General, provided that the third 
						party agrees to protect the confidentiality of your Personally Identifiable 
						Information consistent with this Privacy Policy.
Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy or as otherwise disclosed at the 
						time we request your Personally Identifiable Information, we will not share, 
						rent, sell or trade any of your Personally Identifiable Information with any 
						third party without your permission.

What security does Dollar General use for information collected from me?
We secure your Personally Identifiable Information on servers located in

If you would like to review or correct any Personally Identifiable Information
