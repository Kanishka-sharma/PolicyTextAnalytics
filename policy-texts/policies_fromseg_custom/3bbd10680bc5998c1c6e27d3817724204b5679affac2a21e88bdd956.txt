ETARGET SE Privacy Policy
This policy covers use of personal information that is collected when you provide ETARGET SE with personal information including but not limited to when you use the Website and the collection of information when you are exposed to advertisements served using our platform or technology.
ETARGET SE is a member of IAB Europe’s advertisingconsent.eu and listed as vendor in https://advertisingconsent.eu/transparency-consent-framework-global-vendor-list.
We are fully compliant with the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

3.
==========
Storage of Your Personal Information
ETARGET SE never stores personal information such as name, adress, telephone number or email as part of our advertising technology platform.
Any data used in or stored by our technology platform is always anonymous and does not contain personal information.
Cookie data or IP address is for example never connected to your name, address or email.
Furthermore, IP addresses are shortened before they are stored to remove any detailed identification.
We require our technology partners and clients to make sure any data used in connection with our technology platform is also anonymous.
The only situation we store personal information such as your name is when you register into our system as partner through a “Registration” form on one of our websites or sign up to a communication service with us such as a newsletter.
When you supply any personal information to ETARGET SE, we have legal obligations towards you in the way we use this data.
==========
We must collect the information fairly, that is, we must explain how we will use it, and we will not pass the information on to anyone else.
When using your personal information, ETARGET SE will act in accordance with current legislation and is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.
We will ensure that all personal information supplied is held securely, in accordance with the Personal Data Act.
4.
Use of Your Personal Information
While we only store personal information for the purpose of billing or managing your partner account, any anonymous information we hold for the purpose of operating our technology platform and serve advertisements on behalf of our clients or partners will only be used within ETARGET SE and by our clients or partners for the purposes of providing our services.
We will use information held in our technology platform in the following ways:
•    to serve more relevant advertisements from our clients;
•    to ensure that content is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer;
•    to carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you and us; to allow you to participate in interactive features of our service when you choose to do so;
•    to notify you about changes;
If you have signed up to an email newsletter, you can opt-out of such email correspondence at any time by following an unsubscribe link in an email.
==========
Otherwise, your information will only be disclosed where we are obliged or permitted by law.
We will remove information in the event that you request us to discontinue the holding of your data.
5.
==========
IP Addresses and Cookies
Any data we collect about your computer, including where available your IP address, operating system and browser type, is always shortened or simplified to not contain any personal or identifiable information.
For the same reason, we may obtain information using a cookie file which is stored on the hard drive of your computer.
Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer’s hard drive.
When using cookies, without your specific consent we do not use any anonymous identifier technologies.
Cookies help us to deliver a better and more personalized service for our clients.
Cookies enable us to:
•    better match advertisements with the right audience;
•    to limit the number of how much you will see a specific ad;
When using our advertising technology platform, unless you give us specific consent through one of advertisingconsent.eu consent management providers (full list here: http://advertisingconsent.eu/iab-europe-transparency-consent-framework-list-of-registered-cmps), we set only cookies that are essential for our service.
Any identifiable or personal information is stored only after receiving specific consent from you.
You may review or reverse your consent any time on https://etargetnet.com/cookies.php or from any Consent Management Provider.
==========
We use the following domains to place cookies: etargetnet.com, consensus.org
In addition to cookies from the domains listed above, we also allow cookies to be placed by other technology vendors that are closely tied to our service.
These might include but are not limited to Gemius, Google, Facebook, Mookie, The Rubicon Project, Pubmatic, Pulsepoint, AppNexus, Centro, AdOcean, AdForm, Sizmek.
You may refuse to accept cookies by activating the setting on your browser which allows you to refuse the setting of cookies.
==========
However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of our site and services.
Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you visit our site or see advertisements served by our technology.
For more information on cookies, please go to http://www.allaboutcookies.org.

==========

You can use the cookie management tools in your web browser to manage and remove cookies.

More information can also be found here: http://www.youronlinechoices.eu
Visit https://etargetnet.com/cookies.php to manage and review a complete list of cookies that we use.
7.
==========
Access to Your Information
You have the right to request a copy of the information (personal or non-personal) which we hold about you and to have any inaccuracies corrected.
Please address requests to Data Protection Officer, ETARGET SE, Pribinova 25, 81109 Bratislava, Slovakia.
You have the right to request rectification of your personal data if the information is incorrect, including the right to have incomplete personal data completed.
You have the right to erase any personal data processed by ETARGET SE at any time except for the following situations:
•    you have an ongoing matter with Customer Service
•    you have an open order 
•    you have an unsettled debt with ETARGET SE 
•    if you are suspected or have misused our services within the last four years
•    if you have made any purchase, we will keep your personal data in connection to your transaction for book-keeping purposes
You have the right to object to direct marketing, including profiling analysis made for direct marketing purposes.
You can opt out from direct marketing by the following means:
* following the instruction in each marketing emails
* by editing the settings of your ETARGET SE account
We take data protection very seriously and therefore we have dedicated Data Protection Officers to handle your requests in relation to your rights stated above.
You can always reach them at dpo@etarget.eu.
