Message to the Deakin community

		It may be of use for you to know a little about this site.
The front page of the site has several designs, set to be random for each new user.
This design will then follow on throughout the site.
To see the other designs you can go back to the main page and use the 'reload' button.
==========
This is only the first stage of this new re-development.
Coming stages include complete personalisation of the site, including the ability to structure the pages yourself, adding your own links, integration with email and calendaring systems, dynamic student timetables and much more.
==========
To give us feedback on this stage of the development please use the 'feedback' link on the left hand menu.
To suggest additions for the second stage please send them in an email to info@deakin.edu.au.
==========
