Cookies
At times, we may use a feature of your browser to send and store on your computer or other device a “cookie”.
A cookie is a small data file that assigns a unique anonymous number to your browser from a web server and is stored on your computer’s hard drive.
Cookies make your web experience more enjoyable by storing for example passwords and preferences.
You can adjust your browser settings to refuse all cookies or to inform you when a cookie is being placed on your hard drive.
However, if you elect not to accept cookies your experience with the Service may be diminished and certain functionalities and features of the Service may not function properly.
In addition, the Service may from time to time make use of certain services made available by non-affiliated third parties and such third parties may place their own cookies on your computer or device.
