Non- Personally Identifiable Information Collected.
The Associated Content Network automatically tracks and collects certain information about your visit that does not generally identify you individually.
Similar to other commercial web sites, the Associated Content Network uses standard web measurement and tracking technologies like "cookies," (see explanation below, "Cookies"), web server logs, or other statistics programs, which keep records of traffic on the Associated Content Network.
This data is generally anonymous information that does not personally identify you, e.g., "clickstream data," such as the domain name of the site providing you with Internet access, the Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, your browser type and version, type of operating system and platform you use, the average user session length, the number of hits and pages viewed, top search engines and keywords, access times and other relevant statistics.
Associated Content and/or its web service provider use this information in the aggregate to measure the use of the Associated Content Network and to administer the Associated Content Network.
We also use this information to improve your online experience.
==========
"Cookies" are small pieces of information that a web site transfers to a visitor's hard drive.
Your World Wide Web browser may have an option that allows you to accept cookies, reject cookies, or receive notification when a cookie is sent, but you should note that the use of restrictive browser settings may limit the functionality available to your browser when using the Associated Content Network.
Any cookies and other Internet traffic tracking technologies utilized in connection with the Associated Content Network are not designed to gather any personally identifiable information.
In addition, the Associated Content Network may utilize use web beacons (also known as "action tags" or "one pixel .
gif files"), provided by our advertising serving partner, to measure the effectiveness of, and otherwise manage, our online advertising.
==========
These web beacons enable our advertising server to recognize a browser's cookie when a browser visits the Associated Content Network and to learn which banner ads bring users to the Associated Content Network.
Any information collected using these web beacons or action tags is not personally identifiable and unless otherwise notified, our advertising server does not link online actions to an identifiable person.
