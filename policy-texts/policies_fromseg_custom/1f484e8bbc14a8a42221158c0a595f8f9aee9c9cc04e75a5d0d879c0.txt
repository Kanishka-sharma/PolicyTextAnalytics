Cookies
We use cookies on our Site.
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your hard drive whenever you visit our Site.
==========
These files identify your computer and record your preferences and other data about your visit and about the way you use our Site and Services, including tracking usage on our Site (e.g. information about the sections you visit and the frequency with which you visit various parts of our Site) so that when you return to our Site, it knows who you are and can personalise your visit.
By ticking the check box 'I agree that Hatena may collect, use and process my personal data in accordance with the Privacy Policy' during your registration, you give your consent to us combining this information with your Personal Information for marketing and for market research purposes, including internal demographic studies, so that we can improve and optimise the quality of our Services, customise our Services for you, confirm when you have logged into the Services, measure the number of unique Users of the Services, and for User authentication.
==========
Most browsers are automatically set to accept cookies.
You can disable cookies or you can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent.
You can do this through your browser settings.
==========
If you disable cookies, you may not be able to use all functions of our Site and Services.
Advertisers on our Site may also set cookies.
The Company has no control over such cookies, which are governed by the privacy policies of such parties.
Hatena.com also utilises third-party cookies and image files (web beacons) in order to anonymously track and analyse website usage and activity.
This information is compiled and analysed on an aggregate basis.
