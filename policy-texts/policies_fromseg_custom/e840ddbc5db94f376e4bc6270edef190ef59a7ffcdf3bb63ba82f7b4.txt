Cookies policy

We use cookies in order to provide you with a high-quality and personalised experience.
A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer, laptop or mobile device when you visit a website or use applications.
Cookies may be used for many different purposes such as remembering your preferences, following your use of our website and improving your user experience.
We use three different types of cookies:
Essential Cookies- These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly or to provide a service which you request.
==========
These cookies are usually only set in response to your actions, such as in the case of auto form filling or logging in.
Your browser can be set to block these cookies but the website will not perform properly if you decide to do so.
Functionality Cookies – These cookies help to provide you with a more personalised user experience and are used by us and select third parties whose services feature on our pages.
==========
Blocking these cookies will disable these services and may affect your Website experience.
Performance Cookies – These cookies collect information about how you use a website or application and assist us in improving the performance of our website.
Blocking these cookies means that we cannot work effectively to improve the usability of our website.
Cookies may be stored either for the duration of your visit to the website or for repeat visits:
Session Cookies – These cookies allows us to link your actions during a browser session.
==========
A browser session starts when you open the browser window and finishes when you close it.
Session cookies are created temporarily and are deleted once the browser window is closed.
Persistent Cookies – These cookies remains on your device for a set period of time specified in the cookie and activated each time you visit the website that created that particular cookie.
You may modify your browser so that it will not accept cookies.
Please see the “Help” section of your browser for instructions.
