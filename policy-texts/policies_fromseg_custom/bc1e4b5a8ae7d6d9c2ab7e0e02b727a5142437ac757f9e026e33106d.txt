HOW?
==========
How does Payoneer protect my personal information?
Payoneer restricts access to non-public personal information only to employees and entities on a need-to-know basis who are performing services on our behalf.
We train our employees on the importance of customer privacy and confidentiality.
We also maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal standards to guard the non-public personal information.
