Cookies
Cookies are small packets of data that are stored on your computer’s hard drive so that your computer will “remember” information about your visits to websites or your viewing of ads.
If you do not want us or our service providers to place a cookie on your hard drive, you may be able to turn that feature off in your browser settings or opt out of the use of cookies on your device.
Detailed information about managing cookies on your devices is provided in our Cookie Policy.
==========
Pixel Tags
We or our third party service providers also use pixel tags, which are small, invisible 1x1 images on our Sites.
Pixel tags allow the web page or a third party who serves the pixel tag to set, read and modify cookies.
Our pixel tag allows Beeswax cookies to be set, read, and modified when you visit the websites or apps of our partners.

Please click our opt out here if you'd like to opt-out from having Beeswax collect your information in connection with our technology.
When you opt out, an opt-out cookie will be placed on your computer.
This cookie tells us or the third party that owns the pixel not to collect your information for tailored online advertising campaigns.
