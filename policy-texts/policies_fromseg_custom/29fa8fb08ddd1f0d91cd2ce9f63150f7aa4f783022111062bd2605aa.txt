Privacy Policy


Your privacy on the Internet is very important to us, and we feel you should know how we use the information you provide to us.
Information you provide when using The Globe and Mail Web site is stored securely by Globe Interactive.
We may use the information you provide in the following ways.
==========
To give the producers and developers of the site information that is useful in determining appropriate new features, content and services.
To provide advertisers with aggregate - not individual - information about our user base.
==========
This is essential to providing our service free of charge.
Information about individual users is not shared with any third party.
==========
The Globe and Mail Web site contains links to our advertisers' Web sites.
Globe Interactive is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these Web sites.
If, in a request for information or service or in a contest entry, you indicated you would like us to keep you updated on new site developments, The Globe and Mail will send you these updates by e-mail.
If you decide at any time that you do not want to receive these updates, just send an e-mail message to comments@globeandmail.ca indicating your preference not to receive updates.
