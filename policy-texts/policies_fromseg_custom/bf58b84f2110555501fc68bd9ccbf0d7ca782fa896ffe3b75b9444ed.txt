Privacy Policy
At Friv, the privacy of our visitors is extremely important to us.
This privacy policy document outlines the types of information received and collected by Friv and how it is used.
Log Files
Like many other websites, Friv makes use of server log files.
The log files record data such as the internet protocol (IP) addresses of our users and pages viewed.
Although each user will have a unique IP address, this is never linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Log files are especially useful in identifying errors with our website and / or games.
==========
Cookies / Flash Shared Objects
To help provide an enhanced experience, Friv stores some information about user preferences, browser language, and the number of games played etc.
The games on Friv may also store data for such purposes as recording user preferences, and saving level progress etc.
Unless a user specifically enters personal data, no data stored is personally identifiable.
Tracking / Web Beacons
Sometimes our navigation system and / or the games on Friv will send information to third-party tracking services.
This information, while never personally identifiable, is useful in improving the quality of our website and the games.
You may block third-party tracking services through your individual browser options.
==========
Google AdSense / DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Friv.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to Friv and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy here.
Other Advertising Networks
From time-to-time Friv may use third party ad networks, either via Google or separately.
==========
We have limited control over these networks and no access to any cookies they may use.
If you wish to disable or selectively turn off cookies from these ad networks, you may do so through your individual browser options.
Additionally, you can opt out of the cookie functions of many of these networks by clicking here.
==========
Help Using Friv
To play the games on Friv you should ensure that you have the latest Adobe Flash Player plugin installed and check that you have Javascript enabled.
For additional gameplay, you may also wish to install Adobe Shockwave Player
Unfortunately Friv cannot generally be accessed from handheld mobile devices, although compatibility continually improving.
==========
If you are using a proxy server to access Friv simply because the website has been blocked by your internet provider, you might try accessing www.friv.co.uk instead.
If you are seeing this message because you are viewing Friv from within another (non-proxy) website then please simply click here ...good luck!
