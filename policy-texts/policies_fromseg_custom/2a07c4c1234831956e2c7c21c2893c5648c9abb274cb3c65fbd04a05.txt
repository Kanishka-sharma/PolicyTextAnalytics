When you use this site (Site), the Regents of the University of Michigan (we, our) may collect information in two ways.
We collect and store information that you voluntarily provide when you fill out forms on the Site.
When you access and use the Site, our technology and tools automatically collect and store some non-personally identifiable information about your use of the site (including, for example, your IP address, location information, your browser type, the type of device you used to access the Site, unique device identifiers, and other information about how you use the Site).
==========
We strive to protect the privacy and confidentiality of your data in compliance with federal and state laws, as well as university policy.
The information we collect from this site is considered an institutional asset and may be accessed and used only by authorized University entities according to procedures identified by the University.
No person or entity may access this information, except through strict policies and processes established by the University or by process of law.
==========
We collect information through this site as part of our admissions programs, as well as broader University teaching, learning, scholarly, and research initiatives.
We will not sell the information we collect nor will we use it for commercial purposes.
If you are under 18 years of age, please be sure that your parent or guardian is aware that you are using this Site.
