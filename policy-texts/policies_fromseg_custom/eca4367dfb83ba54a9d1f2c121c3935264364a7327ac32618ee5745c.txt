A session cookie disappears after you close your browser.
A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Website.
Persistent cookies can be removed.
Please review your web browser "Help" file directions, in order to learn the proper way to modify your settings.
You may also set your web browser to refuse cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
