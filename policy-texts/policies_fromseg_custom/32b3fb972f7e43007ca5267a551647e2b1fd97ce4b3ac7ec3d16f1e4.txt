Consumer Privacy.
Blis is integrated with AdChoices and is part of the TrustArc AdChoices programme providing consumers an opportunity to opt-out from behavioural retargeting.
Blis complies with the Children's Online Privacy Act (COPPA) and does not collect any data from those devices that have selected Do Not Track (DNT) or Limited Ad Tracking (LAT).
