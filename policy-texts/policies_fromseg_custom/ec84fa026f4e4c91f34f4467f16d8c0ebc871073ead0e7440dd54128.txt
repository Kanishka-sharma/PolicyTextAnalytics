The United States Department of Commerce and the European Commission have agreed on a set of data protection principles and frequently asked questions (the "Safe Harbor Principles") to enable U.S. companies to satisfy European Union law requirements for adequate protection of personal information transferred from the EU to the United States.
The EEA also has recognized the U.S. Safe Harbor as providing adequate data protection.
Consistent with its commitment to protect personal privacy, Bazaarvoice adheres to the Safe Harbor Principles.
