DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE:
Evite provides Users with a variety of online services, including but not limited to:
a. Private Events.
A private event is a private social gathering or get-together hosted and attended by a group of people in the same social network (friends, family and/ or associates).
If you want to send an Evite invitation to a private event, you must be registered with Evite (see Registration above).
In addition, if you have a My Info Page (see above) your My Info Page information will be viewable to all Guests.
==========
b. Public Events.
A Public Event is a social gathering or get-together that is open to all members of the Evite community.
All Public Events are searchable on the Evite web site.
Some examples of Public Events may include: art exhibits, music festivals, happy-hours, poetry readings, grand openings of restaurants and clubs, etc.
==========
You must create a My Info Page in order to post a Public Event.
If you wish to respond to an Evite invitation to a Public Event, you must first register with Evite.
However, you may choose to respond to an invitation to a Public Event anonymously.
If you choose to respond anonymously, your contact information, user name and comments will be seen only by the Host.
You do not have to create a My Info page in order to respond to a invitation to a Public Event.
c. Recommendations.
Recommendations are Public Events and Places listed on the Evite service based on information you have supplied to us, such as zip code, interests and keyword searches you have conducted.
==========
d. Contact Manager.
Your Contact Manager consists of contact information such as name, email address and other profile information you have either added yourself or requested from another Evite user.
If you don't already have a person's contact information in your Contact Manager, you will see a link to request information if that person is a registered Evite user.
==========
e. Reviews.
Evite allows you to post reviews of bars, restaurants and other places.
Your reviews are viewable by all Evite registered users, however you may choose to hide your profile by posting an anonymous review.
==========
Evite reserves the right to pull down a review if it contains (1) derogatory language, (2) threats of physical harm, or (3) any other reason Evite determines is necessary to protect the integrity of its service.
In furtherance of this endeavor, the Evite Web site will automatically reject certain profane and otherwise inappropriate words in a User review.
