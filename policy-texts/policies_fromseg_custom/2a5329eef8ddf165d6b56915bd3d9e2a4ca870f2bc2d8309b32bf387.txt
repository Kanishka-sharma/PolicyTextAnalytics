1.1 Use of the plista SitesCertain features of the plista Sites may require you to submit personal data.
“Personal data” is information about personal or factual circumstances of an identified or identifiable individual, that is, all information by which you can be identified.For example, we may collect personal data when you request and use personalized offers on the plista Sites, such as our newsletter, which requires provision of an email address during registration.
If you registered for our newsletter, we transmit your data that is necessary for the sending of the newsletter to the service provider that sends the newsletter for us.
Personal data that you transmit to plista will only be collected, processed and used, as far as necessary for the conclusion, design or amendment of contracts with us about the use of the plista Services, and to allow you to use the plista Sites, and to the extent that statutory disclosure requirements exist or if otherwise permitted by the applicable data protection laws.Moreover, the plista Sites´ server collects certain data, which is automatically transmitted by your browser.
Such data includes the name of your internet service provider and IP address, the address of the website from which you visit the plista Sites, the different pages of the plista Sites visited by you, as well as the date and duration of your visit.

below.1.3 Google AnalyticsThe plista Sites use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. (“Google”).
Google Analytics also uses cookies.
The information generated by the cookies about your use of the plista Sites are usually transmitted to a Google server in the USA and stored there.
As “IP anonymization” is activated for the plista Sites, your IP address will be truncated by Google within Member States of the European Union or within Member States of the Agreement on the European Economic Area, and only in exceptional cases will the full IP address be transmitted to a Google server in the US and be truncated there.
Google will use this information on our behalf to analyze your use of the plista Sites, to compile reports on website activity and to provide services to us related to the use of the internet or the plista Sites.According to Google’s terms, the IP address transmitted by your browser in the context of Google Analytics will not be merged with other data of Google.
You can reject the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser software; however, please note that you may not be able to use all functionalities of the plista Sites to their full extent in this case.
You can also prevent transmission of the data generated by the cookies and relating to your usage of the plista Sites (incl.
your IP address) to Google as well as the processing of these data by Google by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available at the following link: http://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=de.  For information on the data use and retention practices of Google and its affiliates and subsidiaries, please see Google’s privacy policy currently located at www.google.com/privacy.html. 1.4 Disclosure of Personal Data to Third Parties As a matter of principle, we do not share your personal data with third parties unless permitted by law.
