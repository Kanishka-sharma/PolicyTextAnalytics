Information collected during market research
This category of personal data includes all data we collect during a market research (including any media analysis carried out for the benefit of Realeyes’ customers and software improvement)
Such personal data includes the following
• Assignment of unique identifiers during Services engagements, including via cookies.
• Survey responses and market research study data explicitly provided by the subjects of the survey or market research data.
==========
• Your device environment and specifications:
o Platform (mobile/tablet/desktop).
o Browser name and version.
o Operating System name and version.
o Anonymised IP address with the last subnet discarded.
==========
• Your interactions with the content via the device:
o Whether a video loads and is in focus.
o How you engage with a video.
o Video metadata such as content ID, title, and category.
o Percentage of video content that has been viewed.
• Facial recordings of you as you interact with the media content, via the front-facing camera on your device:
If you have given us permission to use the front-facing camera on your device then, while you are interacting with the content, we access the data from the camera and process it on your device using our software to derive the following separate data-points:
o Whether a face is detected and, if so, the position and orientation of the face relative to the screen.
Our software does not engage in facial recognition of any kind.
o The extent to which you appear to be paying attention to the content.
o Your apparent reaction to the content (levels of overall emotion displayed, such as happiness, surprise, sadness, feat, confusion, contempt or disgust).
• Data we have accessed from the camera will be used strictly for reporting to our customers and for enhancements in the use of software.
