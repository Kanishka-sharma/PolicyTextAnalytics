About the Site: Privacy Statement








The following explains how inc.com collects information from you and what we do with it.
Information Requests

In order to make full use of the resources on the site, you may be asked to provide us with information.
For example, if you wish to receive newsletters, you will be asked to provide your e-mail address so that we can send 
the requested newsletters to you.
You also may receive promotional e-mail that relates to the subject of the newsletters.
==========
If you wish to participate in discussions, create BizCards or take advantage of the opportunity to personalize the site so 
that it is more adapted to your needs, you will be asked to register for My inc.com.
Registration for My inc.com require 
that you choose a user name and password so that only you can control the information provided by or about you on the site.
Registration also requires you to provide your e-mail address, name, company name and address and identifying information 
about your company.
==========
Inc.com may, unless you choose to opt out, use this information to send you promotional material for goods 
and services that we think may interest you.
You will be asked for similar information as well as credit card information if 
you wish to make purchases from inc.com.
This information will be used to fill you order and your credit card information 
will be provided to a third party solely for authentication and processing.
==========
If you contact a Solutions Provider from the site, 
the Solutions Provider will also request information from you to complete its sales process.
Sharing Information

Inc.com shares registration information on users who have purchased goods or services from that Solutions Provider and, 
in turn, receives information from the Solutions Provider on transactions which you enter into with them.
This information may be used to identify users to whom inc.com sends additional information and product promotions.
Inc.com does not otherwise provide the identifying information we collect from you to third parties.
Choice to Opt Out

You may, if you wish, have inc.com use the e-mail address your have provided for newsletter delivery only to send you those 
newsletters you have requested.
You will be given the opportunity to indicate that you do not wish to continue to receive 
a newsletter.
==========
Marketers unaffiliated with inc.com often collect the e-mail addresses of those who choose to participate in discussions which 
are hosted by inc.com, but inc.com can not control their doing so.
Inc.com does use information collected in the My inc.com registration process and from purchaser in aggregate form.
==========
That information may be provided to advertisers and others.
If and when this is done, inc.com will not include any identifiable 
individual information.
This compilation of data may be used for various internal business purposes and will be made available 
to users receiving Smart Statements.
==========
Credit Card Transactions

If you use your credit card to purchase products or services from inc.com, the transactions will be carried out through a secure 
server using 128-bit encryption.
You will be able to tell that you are using a secure server for the transaction because the web 
address will begin with "https" (the added "s" indicates that the transaction is secure).
Your credit card information will 
be secure from outside third parties because it is encrypted.
==========
Inc.com has also circulated security policies to all employees 
and reminds employees of these policies on a regular basis, not less than once every calendar quarter.
Only customer service 
representatives will have access to credit card information in readable form and they are bound by confidentiality agreements 
not to disclose or use that information.
==========
Cookies

All visitors to inc.com are issued a "cookie"--a small text file that inc.com places on your hard drive in order to prevent your 
repeatedly seeing the same ads or being asked to register more than once.
Cookies are also used to authenticate your identity.
==========
We have asked Solution Providers to issue a cookie to visitors who have been referred to them through the inc.com site.
This will allow you to be recognized as an inc.com customer and enable each Solution Provider to monitor its transactions with 
inc.com customers and report that information to us.
==========
A cookie is specific to the server that delivered it.
It can not be used by us or others to track your activities on the web.
==========
No cookie delivered by inc.com will interfere with the operation of your computer but if you disable the cookie, inc.com will not 
be able to identify you as a registered user or a repeat customer and you will have to reregister or supply all personal information 
each time you use My inc.com functions or purchase products from inc.com.
Banner Ads

Inc.com uses DoubleClick Company ("DoubleClick") to serve banner adds on its site.
Banner ads may contain cookies.
While we use cookies in the manner described above, cookies received by you from banner ads are collected by DoubleClick 
and we do not have access to this information.
User information may be shared with DoubleClick or other companies serving 
ads on the site, but only in aggregate form.
We encourage you to visit the 
site to learn how information it gathers is used by DoubleClick.
==========
Various

We have endeavored to have Solutions Providers adopt appropriate privacy and security policies, but inc.com can not control their 
actions or be responsible for their use of information that you provide to them.
You should inform yourself concerning the 
privacy policy of each Solution Provider with whom you deal.
==========
For a fuller description of their relationship with inc.com, 
click on Solutions Provider.
For a disclaimer of inc.com's responsibility for Solution Providers' products, services and actions, 
click on Legal Disclaimers and see Solution Providers.
Inc.com maintains site logs that permit it to count visitors and page views and to analyze the number of visitors who click 
through to advertisers and Solutions Providers.
==========
This information is in statistical form only and is not preserved in a form 
that allows us to track the activities of individual users.
All requested information must be supplied in order to register for My inc.com or to make purchases from us.
Inc.com has strategic alliances with other Internet sites to which it provides content or through which it promotes the sale 
of products or the products and services of Solutions Providers.
These alliances may lead users to link to the inc.com site 
from elsewhere but no user information is provided to inc.com as a result of the linking.
==========
That is, registration or purchase 
information is provided by the user only to inc.com and is subject to these privacy policies.
The third parties to whom inc.com provides credit card information for authentication and processing use it only for those 
functions and do not store the information we provide to them.
My inc.com registrants and purchasers from inc.com will be given the opportunity to update registration material at each sign 
on and at the time of each subsequent purchase.
==========
If inc.com changes its privacy policies, it will advise each newsletter recipient, 
each My inc.com registrant and each purchaser of the change in its policies at the time the change is made.
inc.com has taken reasonable steps to limit the uses of information provided by you to those described in this Privacy Statement 
but it can not guarantee that the information will be immune to exposure because of technical problems or the deliberate actions 
of unauthorized intruders into the web site.
inc.com is committed to maintain the trust of our users.
If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy or security of 
any information that you have provided to inc.com, you can reach us by clicking on Contact Us.
