Orkut Privacy Policy

Scope

							Welcome to orkut.com, a 
							personal online networking service operated by orkut.com LLC 
							("orkut").
The Google Privacy Policy (http://www.google.com/privacy.html) 
							describes how we treat personal information when you use Google services.
In addition, the following describes practices specific to orkut.
By using orkut, you understand that this policy applies to you.
If we make any material changes to this policy, we will 
							notify you by posting a notice of such changes here.
==========
You should know:
							

We collect, store and maintain account information on our secure servers.
You can update your account information and preferences/settings at any time.
We will never share personal information with unrelated third parties for marketing purposes without your express permission.
As described in the orkut terms of service, orkut is only available to users who are at least 18 years old.
Personal Information

							Account information.
To use orkut, you need to have a Google Account.
When you sign up for a Google Account, we will save your username and password so that you can log in to orkut and other Google services.
Profile information.
When you register to become a member of orkut, we collect your name, email address, and other 
							personal identifying information that you submit to the site.
As part of your 
							personal profile, you may choose to submit additional information, including 
							your gender, age, occupation, hobbies, and interests, plus other content, such as photos.
When you invite new members into your network 
							or send messages through orkut, we collect and maintain information associated 
							with those messages, including email addresses and content.
As with any social 
							networking service, you should use care when disclosing any personal 
							information to strangers.
==========
Uses

							We display the profile information described 
							above according to the preferences you set in your account.
The name you choose 
							to display will be posted with your profile summary and will be available to 
							other orkut users.
Be advised that if you post material containing personal 
							information, that information may be used and collected by others.
==========
When you send messages through orkut, your 
							name and email address will be identified in the message.
We also use your name and email address to 
							notify you of new members, messages, or other information, such as invitations 
							to join friends' networks, new testimonials or "fan" ratings, and crush 
							notices.
We will send you messages when there are 
							substantive changes to the service or our policies.
==========
Over time, orkut may use 
							additional information about your activity on Google or other information you 
							provide us in order to provide a better orkut experience.
If you invite another person to join orkut, we 
							use the name and email address you provide us to contact that person and, if 
							necessary, remind that person that he or she has been invited to join orkut.
All invitees are given the option not to receive further invitations from 
							orkut.
==========

								subsidiaries, affiliated companies or other trusted businesses or persons for

We do this 
								only when those parties agree to process such information only on our

								confidentiality and security measures.
We may share personal information you 
								provide when registering f or an account with certain Google services
								among other Google services; for

We have a good faith belief that access, 
								use, preservation or disclosure of such information is reasonably necessary to 
								(a) satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental

==========
If Google becomes involved in a merger, 
								acquisition, or any form of sale of some or all of its assets, we will provide 
								notice before personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a 
								different privacy policy.
Transfer of information.
Personal information collected by orkut and 
							Google may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in 
							which Google Inc. or its agents maintain facilities.
We reserve the right to transfer your personal 
							information in the event of a transfer of ownership of Google or orkut, such as 
							acquisition by or merger with another company.
In such an event, Google will 
							notify you before information is transferred and becomes subject to a different 
							privacy policy.
==========
Security

							We use industry standard security measures to 
							protect your information so that it is not made available to unauthorized 
							parties.
Your account and personal information will be stored on Google and 
							orkut secure servers.
Your Choices

							Orkut provides a number of tools to limit who 
							can view your profile and other personal information.
When you create your 
							profile, look for the "golden key" icon, which allows you to limit viewing 
							certain information to yourself, your friends, friends of friends, or to make 
							the information available to all orkut members.
==========
You can update your profile information at any 
							time by clicking on the "edit" button in your profile setting, and your changes 
							will take effect immediately.
Messages you receive from orkut.com can be 
							sent to your primary email address or orkut inbox, depending on the preferences 
							you choose in the "my settings" page.
You can terminate your account at any time.
To 
							learn how, 
click here.
If you 
							terminate your account, your profile, including any messages in your inbox, 
							will be removed from the site and deleted from orkut servers.
Because of the 
							way we maintain this service such deletion may not be immediate, and residual 
							copies of your profile information may remain on backup media.
You should be 
							aware that any content you created while using orkut, including orkut 
							communities, email messages sent to other members, or comments made to other 
							members' profiles will remain on orkut even after the termination of your 
							account.
