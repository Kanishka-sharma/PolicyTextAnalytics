General Privacy Policy
Fatakat.com is keen to protect your privacy.
Here are our privacy policy in points:
Upon your registration to fatakat.com, you provide your email, job title, social status, city and country.

These data are collected in order to make everyone in this community more aware of others and more familiar to them.
Such data is shown on your listing page at fatakat.com.
