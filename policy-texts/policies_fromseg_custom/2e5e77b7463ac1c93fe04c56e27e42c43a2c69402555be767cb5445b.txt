Information We Obtain
We receive information about users of News Corporation brands and properties, including personal information, from our Company affiliates and their sites and applications (a current list of our participating affiliated companies is available at here; surveys and publicly and commercially available sources; social media networks; and in the future, of users of business partner’s sites and applications that are not affiliated with us.
==========
Personal Information
The types of personal information the Company may obtain include but are not limited to name, postal and/or billing address, email address, telephone and/or mobile numbers, geolocation, and username (“Personal Information”).
Other Data
We may obtain other information provided by you to the Company or our affiliates such as date of birth, preferences, demographical and occupational data.
You may provide this information, for example, when registering for or using Company affiliate services.
NCAE may also obtain content provided by you, such as survey responses, reviews and comments; information provided to us by social networks when you visit Company affiliate social media platforms, social networking sites or otherwise register on a Company affiliate site or application through your social network account (such as name, likes, location, friends list and other information described when you connect with the social networking platform).
The specific information we obtain depends on your privacy settings on the applicable social media network.
All of this information is referred to as “Other Data”.
==========
Activity Information
We also obtain certain information by automated means, such as cookies (including HTTP, HTML5 and Flash cookies), web beacons, web server logs, JavaScript and similar technologies, including technologies designed to obtain information regarding your usage of various Company affiliate sites and applications, and other offerings and services.
A “cookie” is a file that websites send to a user’s computer or other Internet-connected device to identify the user’s browser or to store information or settings in the browser.
A “web beacon” also known as an Internet tag, pixel tag or clear GIF, links web pages to web servers and their cookies and may be used to transmit information collected through cookies back to a web server.
The information the Company obtains through these automated collection methods may include IP address, mobile device advertising ID, browser characteristics, device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, information on actions taken on Company affiliate sites, and dates and times users access or use Company affiliate sites.
We may link certain Activity Information, such as browser or device information, with other Activity Information, such as whether an email sent to you has been opened, what search queries you may have run, and what advertisements you may have seen on Company affiliate sites.
We also may use similar automated means to obtain and store similar information regarding user’s actions while using Company affiliate mobile applications.
All of this information is known as “Activity Information”.
You also may be able to delete Flash cookies or adjust Flash cookie settings by visiting the Adobe Flash Website Storage Settings Panel and Global Storage Settings Panel.
==========
Please note, however, that without cookies some or all of the features of Company affiliate services may not be available to you.
User device settings also may limit mobile application platforms (such as Apple and Google) from sharing certain information obtained by automated means with us through our Company affiliate applications.
How We Use the Information We Obtain
We use the information collected from and about you to:
Supplement Personal Information collected by the Company and our affiliates with additional information from publicly and commercially available sources, and make inferences regarding your preferences and further supplementing your profile with such inferred preferences to provide relevant content and advertising;
Display targeted advertising and otherwise send promotional materials, alerts regarding available offers and other communications, including communications through our Company affiliate sites and applications, and via email;
Customize your visits to and use of our Company affiliate sites and applications, and deliver content (including advertising) tailored to your interests and the manner in which you use our Company affiliate sites and applications;
Communicate about, and administer participation in, special events, promotions, programs, offers, surveys, contests and market research;
Anonymize or pseudonymize Personal Information to provide third parties with aggregated data reports showing anonymized or pseudonymized information and other non-Personal Information;
Associate your browser and/or device with other browsers or devices you use for the purpose of providing relevant and easier access to content, advertising across browsers and devices, and other operational/business purposes;
Operate, evaluate and improve our business (including developing, enhancing, analyzing and improving our programs and offerings; managing our communications; performing data analytics; and performing accounting, auditing and other internal functions);
Protect against, identify and prevent fraud and other unlawful activity, claims and other liabilities; and
Comply with and enforce applicable legal requirements, relevant industry standards, contractual obligations and our policies.
==========
We also may use your Personal Information in other ways for which we provide specific notice at the time of collection.
In addition, when the information collected from or about you does not identify you as a specific person, we may use it in any lawful manner.

Interest-Based Advertising
On our Company affiliate sites, applications and/or emails, we and our service providers obtain information about your activities to provide you with advertising about products and services tailored to your individual interests.
News Corp Connect serves as an advertising exchange to help our advertisers target their messaging to users through interest-based and contextual means.
==========
Throughout our advertising exchange for our Company affiliate sites and applications, we can gather information about online activities over time and across Company affiliate sites and applications by obtaining Personal Information, Other Data and Activity Information, as described above.
Our exchange uses all such information to show users advertisements on our Company affiliate sites and applications that aim to be tailored to your individual interests.
Collection of Activity Information, Personal Information and Other Data currently takes place on Company affiliate sites, applications and emails that participate in our advertising exchange.
This process also helps us track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns conducted through the advertising exchange.
==========
The Company adheres to the Digital Advertising Alliances Self-Regulatory Principles and provides users choice with respect to the Company advertising exchange interest-based advertising.
To learn how to opt out of the News Corp Connect interest-based advertising see the section on Your Choices below.
==========
In the future, the Company may expand its services and serve as a third-party advertising exchange, at which point, all the same information and data as described in this Notice will be obtained by NCAE from its business partners participating in the third-party advertising exchange and used in the same manner and for the same purposes as described in this Notice.
Such data collection and advertising activity will then also take place on our business partner sites, applications and emails.
Users will continue to be provided choice with respect to News Corp Connect interest-based advertising.
