Whois.com is committed to principles of internet security and 
                respect for your privacy.
This privacy policy explains why and 
                when your corporate/personal information is collected and why 
                this information is needed by Whois.com or a third party.
Your corporate/personal information
                In order to register a domain name at Whois.com you are required 
                to provide us with corporate/personal information by way of an 
                electronic online form.
To register a domain name corporate/personal 
                information is required by the relevant registry of the TLD (top 
                level domain) for which you apply to register a domain name through 
                Whois.com.
==========
This information may include but not be limited to 
                company name, full name, mailing address, email address, contact 
                information for the account holder, registrant, billing, administrative, 
                technical contacts and your credit card details.
Security
                In order to protect the security of corporate/personal information 
                and credit card details provided by you through our Web site, 
                our Web servers support the use of the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) 
                Protocol where appropriate.
Using this protocol your credit card 
                details are encrypted when transmitted to the bank.
*Note: Whois.com does not track or store credit card details of 
                its customers.
==========
Use of your information by third parties or as required 
                by the relevant TLD registries.
On occasion, Whois.com may also engage third party companies or 
                individuals to perform services on our behalf.
Our relationships 
                with such third party companies are governed by our contracts 
                with them.
==========
Those companies are required to hold your personal 
                information strictly confidential.
Use of your information by Whois.com 
                We will use your personal information to advise you of any information 
                relating to the services provided to you through Whois.com.
==========
This includes but is not limited to notices for renewal of services, 
                of system upgrades or any information relevant to you.
If you 
                do not wish to receive notices or other communications from Whois.com 
                regarding additional product and service information, simply send 
                an email to unsubscribe@Whois.com.
==========
Corrections or Changes to your corporate/personal Information 
                
                You have the ability to correct or change certain information 
                in our records, such as your address and contact information.
The process for changing your information begins at www.whois.com 
                You may change this information at any time and as often as necessary.
If you need assistance or have questions about correcting information, 
                you can contact us via email at 
                info@whois.com.
*Note: Whois.com may make changes to this privacy policy at anytime, 
                so it is your responsibility to check our privacy policy from 
                time to time.
If you have any question or comment, please contact us at info@whois.com.
==========
