Introduction
admetrics GmbH is an ad verification vendor.
Our goal is to increase the quality of ads and uncover invalid and fraudulent ad traffic.
We appreciate that you are aware of and care about your own personal privacy interests, and take that seriously.
This Privacy Notice outlines our policies and practices regarding its collection and use of your data.
This Privacy Notice is intended to provide you with information about what personal data admetrics GmbH collects about you and how it is used.
If you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@admetrics.io.
Please recognize that information privacy is an ongoing responsibility, and so we will update this Privacy Notice as we develop new personal data practices or adopt new privacy policies.
What personal data do we process or collect?
==========
Our services enable our clients to report on ad delivery and performance by making use of web beacons and log files.
For that purpose we process IP addresses.
To protect your data we anonymize and encrypt your IP address and remove it automatically after 5 days.
Your rights
If you wish to confirm that we are processing your personal data, or to have access to the personal data admetrics may have about you, please contact us at privacy@admetrics.io.
If you wish us to stop collecting and processing anonymized personal data as outlined above, please use the button below to change your processing preference.
==========
Some browsers (like Safari) may prevent us from saving your processing choice.
The easiest and most permanent way for you to store your tracking preference is by enabling or disabling the Do Not Track feature in your web browser:
Chrome: Head into the Settings page and click "Show advanced settings."
Scroll down to the Privacy section and select Do Not Track.
Firefox: Select Preferences > Privacy and check the box marked, "Tell websites I do not want to be tracked."
Internet Explorer: Click the Tools button and then Internet Options > Advanced.
Select "Always send Do Not Track Header."
Safari: Head into Preferences > Privacy and check the box marked "Ask website not to track me."
Mobile Chrome: Head into the Settings and then Privacy > Do Not Track.
