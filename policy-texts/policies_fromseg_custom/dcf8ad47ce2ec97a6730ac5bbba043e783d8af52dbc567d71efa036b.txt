Privacy Policy
This page outlines what personal data is collected by Smash Balloon, how it is stored, whether it is transferred to third-parties, and how to request deletion of this data.
Logging into your Social Media Account through our Plugins
We do not store or collect any data obtained when logging into your Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter account through one of our plugins.
The information obtained through these logins stays on your website and is stored there.
It is not stored on our web server or shared with any other third parties.
==========
Support and Contact Forms
When you submit a request through any of the contact or support forms on our website we ask for your name and email address in order to be able to respond to your request.
This information, along with your public IP address, is stored as a form entry in our database.
The request and your information is also forwarded to our support ticketing helpdesk, powered by Freshdesk (privacy policy).
==========
Third Party Widgets
We make use of certain third-party widgets on our website in order to enhance the experience, namely; YouTube video embeds and social media widgets/buttons.
These widgets may collect your IP address, your web browser User Agent, store and retrieve cookies on your browser, embed additional tracking, and monitor your interaction with the widget, including correlating your social media account with whatever action you take within the widget, if you are logged in to that network.
For more information about how this data may be used, please see the associated data privacy policy below:

Third Party Resources
Our website makes use of several third-party resources for both functional and performance reasons.
Requests to load these resources may make your IP address visible to the associated resource, who may use it in accordance with their data privacy policy.
==========
The third party resources are listed below:

Stripe is a payment processor which allows you to securely and conveniently checkout while purchasing a product on our website.
We utilize their scripts to integrate the payment process into our website.
Stripe’s privacy policy can be found here: https://stripe.com/us/privacy
Gravatar
Gravatar is used to display avatar images of people whose product reviews are displayed on our website.
Privacy policy: https://automattic.com/privacy/
MaxCDN
MaxCDN is a Content Delivery Network which we use to serve certain static files on our website in order to improve performance.
These requests make your IP address visible to MaxCDN, who may use it in accordance with their data privacy policy: https://www.maxcdn.com/legal/#pp
Our Plugin Demos
We display demos of our plugins on our website to allow you to try them out in a limited capacity before deciding whether you’d like to purchase one.
As a result, our website makes requests to the servers of each of the associated social media networks in order to get the data to populate the feed(s) and to display images and videos.
These requests make your IP address visible to that social media network, who may use it in accordance with their data privacy policies:
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/update
Instagram: https://help.instagram.com/519522125107875
Twitter: https://twitter.com/en/privacy#update
The Tweets displayed in the Twitter feed demo include the ability to Reply, Share, or Like the Tweet directly on our website.
If you choose to interact with these functions then you will make a connection to Twitter.com, who may collect your IP address, your web browser User Agent, store and retrieve cookies on your browser, embed additional tracking, and monitor your interaction with the widget, including correlating your Twitter account with whatever action you take within the widget (such as “liking” a Tweet), if you are logged in to Twitter.
==========
For more information about how this data may be used, please see Twitter’s data privacy policy: https://twitter.com/en/privacy#update
Analytics
In order to provide you with both a better website experience and to help improve our products, we use analytics tools to collect basic usage data from our website visitors.
We use Google Analytics to collect general browsing information and data such as an IP address and location.
The Google Analytics privacy policy can be found at the following link: https://www.google.com/analytics/terms/dpa/dataprocessingamendment_20130906.html.  When available, we also track the page history of visitors on our website to allow us to better understand how our website is being used.
These tools may use cookies to help track website usage.
