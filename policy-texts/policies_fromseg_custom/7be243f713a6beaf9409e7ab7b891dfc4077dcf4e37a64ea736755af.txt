PRIVACY POLICY
 
This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices
for Marketing Science Consulting Group, Inc. and the technology toolset
Bot Analytics (b2c.com).
Information Collection, Use, and Sharing 
 
We collect data about the browser using standard javascript
requests, as specified by the W3C.
( https://www.w3.org/standards/webdesign/script
); the same as other analytics platforms such as Google Analytics or Adobe
Analytics.
==========
This data contains NO personal information or personally
identifiable information.
We will never ask you to provide any information to
us.
==========
We do not set or use cookies.
We do not write or store any
information similar to cookies in your browser.
The data enables us to make a determination whether the visitor
is a bot or human.
==========
There is NO login process, usernames, or passwords.
We are the sole owners of the information collected on this
site.
We will not sell or rent this collected
information to anyone.
==========
We use aggregated data to assess the quality of the
entire population of visitors.
Security 

We take precautions to protect the anonymous data that is collected.
==========
We always
use "https" to secure all communications to and from the browser and
our servers.
All data is encrypted during transmission and in storage on the
servers.
Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all
updates will be posted on this page.
