Selecting an Online Privacy Statement
  
  


Determine the appropriate privacy statement to link to or post on the web site.
University of Minnesota online privacy statement (www.privacy.umn.edu) is for sites that collect only routine web site statistics
Customized online privacy statement is for sites that collect other information beyond routine web site statistics.
Customized online privacy statements must include additional sections.
See the Appendix: Language for Customized Privacy Statements for sections to include and sample wording.Link to the privacy statement.
The word Privacy must be included on the bottom of the web page with a link to the appropriate privacy statement for the web site.
