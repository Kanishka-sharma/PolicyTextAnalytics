What do we mean by personal data?
“Personal Data” is defined as any data relating to a living individual who can be identified directly from that data or indirectly in conjunction with other information.
==========
It can take the form of a name or address and, now, under new data protection laws in Europe, this can extend to uniquely generated IDs, IP addresses and other “unique identifiers”.
As part of our Services, we collect and process the following information: IP address, information regarding a user’s device, cookies, HTTP header data.
The combination of this information, with a unique identifier (set by us or a third party) is considered Personal Data.
