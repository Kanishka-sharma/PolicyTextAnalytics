Security

Goldman Sachs is committed to being at the forefront of information security to protect the privacy and confidentiality of your personal information.
Only authorized Goldman Sachs employees or agents will have access to your personal

information.
All of our employees, service providers and marketing partners are held

to stringent standards of privacy and security and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
==========
Users of the non-public areas of this Web site will be required to identify and authenticate themselves prior to accessing our services.
Generally, identification and authentication take place through the use of your user name and a password or with a SecurID Card® and Personal Identification Number (PIN).
The non-public areas of this site are built upon a secure infrastructure with multiple layers of protection, including measures ranging from proper physical security of our machines to system intrusion detection capabilities.
Within those non-public areas, Goldman Sachs makes use of industry-standard Secure Socket Layer and encryption technologies to protect your information from external compromise.
Security is a cooperative effort between Goldman Sachs and the users of this site.
Please remember that your password, SecurID Card and PIN are personal to you and should not be made available to any other person.
==========
Also, you should discontinue their use and advise us if you have any reason to suspect that someone else may be using them.
