Information Collection Disclosure
					



						Registration
					


					During registration you are required to give contact information such as first and last name, email address, university (if applicable), zip code, gender as well as a protected password.
==========
We ask that you provide demographic information (such as your university, gender and level) to us so we can provide you a more personalized experience on our site.
Chegg also provides users with the option of entering school/class information which is used to help customers find the correct textbooks for their classes.
School information is displayed in the public/private section, however your profile information will only be provided as authorized by you in the private section of Chegg.com.
Once a user, you may provide additional information in the profile section describing your contact information, privacy filters and student information and billing and shipping address changes.
Providing additional information beyond what is required at registration is entirely optional, but enables you to better identify yourself and helps Chegg find the textbooks you want.
==========
