What this Privacy Policy covers
This Privacy Policy covers Smaato treatment of personally identifiable information that Smaato collects when you are on the Smaato site, and when you use Smaato services such as mobile applications.

Information Collection and Use
Smaato collects personally identifiable information when you register for a Smaato account, when you use certain Smaato products or services and when you visit Smaato pages.

When you register with Smaato, we ask for a certain amount of personal information.

Smaato uses information for three general purposes: to constantly improve its services and products, to fulfill your requests regarding its products and to contact you about special offers and new products.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
