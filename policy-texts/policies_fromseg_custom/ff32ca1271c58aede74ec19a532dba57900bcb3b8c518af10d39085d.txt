Optimizely is responsible for the processing of personal data it receives under the Privacy Shield and subsequently transfers to a third party acting as an agent on its behalf.

With respect to personal data received or transferred pursuant to the Privacy Shield Framework, Optimizely is subject to the investigatory and enforcement powers of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission.
In certain situations, Optimizely may be required to disclose personal data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.
