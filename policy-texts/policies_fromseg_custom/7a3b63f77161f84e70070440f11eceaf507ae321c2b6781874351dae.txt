Site Visits and Data Security:

research and development,

Use of Cookies
Cookies are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored information
from a Website.
A cookie is an element of data that a Website can send to your browser,
and which your browser may then store on your system.
You can set your browser to notify
you when you receive a cookie.
Doing this will allow you to decide on a case-by-case
basis whether to accept cookies or not.
Cookies on this site are used by ourselves, our ad management system, and occasionally by
advertisers who use 3rd party ad serving solutions.
==========
Cookies are used for: 

ad tracking, 
customization of content (i.e. not repeating pop-up windows), 
speeding up transactions (by storing your email address),
tracking the source of product sales,
storing login information, and
statistical site-traffic analysis.
