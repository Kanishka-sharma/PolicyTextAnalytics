c - category (all lowercase, "-" separator) Example: c=big-dick
t - tag (all lowercase, "-" separator) Example: t=outdoor

u - submitter name (all lowercase, "-" separator) Example: u=brazzers-network
n - number of results in the feed (max: 25) Example: n=15
