Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to us.
To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.
==========
On some pages, you may enter your email adress (to register for a member account).
We use the email you provide for notification of a response at a message you post on the forum, for notification of news, or to get in touch with the font authors.
==========
We never use or share the email adress provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above.
Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.
==========
We may use cookies to improve your web browsing experience, for example to avoid you typing your login/email adress at each comment you post, or to keep your color settings.
Some outside parties (advertisers) may use cookies to understand the way a user responds to the ads they deliver.
==========
For example, to let them keep track of how many times you view an ad they distribute.
Likewise, the cookie can help make sure you do not see the same ad over and over.
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email.
==========
