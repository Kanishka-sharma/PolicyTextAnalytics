At PartyPoker.com,
                we respect your privacy and are committed to keeping personal
                information about you strictly between us.
PartyPoker.com
                aims to provide you with the highest levels of privacy available
                from any Internet business in the world.
For the security of all
                of our clients, we have adopted our own Privacy Code.
==========
Personally identifiable information
                on individual users will not be sold or otherwise transferred
                to unaffiliated third parties.
We use this information to improve
                our site, provide customer service, to target advertising and
                to track your interest in the content of the site.
==========
PartyPoker.com does not collect personal data that is not relevant
                to providing you with the best service possible.
PartyPoker.com
                strives to keep all personal information accurate, complete and
                up-to-date.
PartyPoker.com will not use your personal data for
                any other purpose without your prior consent.
We do not sell,
                trade, or share the personal information you have entrusted us
                with.
==========
If you provide personal information during an interaction
                with our customer service functions (for example, an e-mail address
                or telephone number), we use it only for the purpose of providing
                you with service.
We will not sell your phone number to telemarketers
                or to anyone else.
==========
PartyPoker.com uses
                reasonable security safeguards to protect Players' personal data
                against such risks as loss or unauthorized access, destruction,
                use, modification or disclosure of data.
All employees, contractors
                and other companies engaged by PartyPoker.com for work
                that involves access to personal information are contractually
                required to keep the information confidential and not to use it
                in any way other than what is necessary to perform their work
                for PartyPoker.com.
The personal data associated with Accounts that have been terminated
                are soon after destroyed or converted into summary statistics
                or anonymous forms where the personal identifiers are removed.
==========
PartyPoker.com will always abide by this Privacy Code.
Occasionally
                some of the details under them may require updating by PartyPoker.com,
                as it deems appropriate in its sole discretion.
In such event,
                PartyPoker.com will update this web page as published here within
                reasonable time.
If you do not agree to the changes, you will
                have the option to close your Account and to tell PartyPoker.com
                to delete your personal information.
==========
