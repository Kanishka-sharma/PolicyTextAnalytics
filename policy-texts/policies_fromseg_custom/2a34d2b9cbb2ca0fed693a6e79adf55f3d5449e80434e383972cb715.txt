Identity Theft

Identity theft is a crime that is growing at an alarming rate.
It is estimated that last year alone, over 700,000 Americans became victims of identity theft.
Identity theft occurs when someone acquires your name, Social Security number, credit card number, or some other piece of your personal information without your knowledge or consent to commit fraud or theft.

Random Term From The Legal Dictionary:

Clandestine
Something that is purposely kept from the view or knowledge of others either in violation of the law or to conduct or conceal some illegal purpose.
A "clandestine marriage" would be one which does not comply with laws related to publicity.
