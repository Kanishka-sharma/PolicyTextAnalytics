Who Is Meredith?
Meredith Corporation is a publicly held media and marketing services company founded upon serving our customers and committed to building value for our shareholders.
Meredith Corporation and our brands focus on providing destinations you can trust and rely on for every stage of your life to connect you to great content, products and services, and a personalized experience.
Below is a list of the brands that are a part of the Meredith Corporation family of companies:
National Media Group.
==========
Meredith’s National Media Group reaches more than 180 million unduplicated American consumers every month, including nearly 90 percent of U.S. millennial women.
Meredith is a leader in creating content across media platforms and life stages in key consumer interest areas such as entertainment, food, lifestyle, home, parenting, beauty, and fashion.

Media Brands: Such as PEOPLE, Better Homes & Gardens, Allrecipes, Entertainment Weekly, Real Simple and more than twenty others.

The Foundry and Foundry360.
-The Foundry is Meredith’s state-of-the-art creative lab and content studio.

Local Media Group.

Meredith adheres to the principles of the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework (“Privacy Shield Principles”) as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information transferred from the European Union (“EU”) to the United States.

If there is any conflict between the terms in this Privacy Statement and the Privacy Shield Principles, the Privacy Shield Principles will govern.
To learn more about the Privacy Shield program, please visit the Privacy Shield website.
Meredith is responsible for the processing of personal information it receives under the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework, or subsequently transfers to a third party acting as an agent on its behalf.
Meredith complies with the Privacy Shield Principles for all onward transfers from the EU and European Economic Area (“EEA”), including onward transfer liability provisions.
The U.S. Federal Trade Commission has jurisdiction over Meredith’s compliance with its obligations under the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield.

If you do not receive timely acknowledgment of your complaint from us, or if we have not resolved your complaint, you may submit your complaint at no cost to you to JAMS, our independent recourse mechanism, at https://www.jamsadr.com/eu-us-privacy-shield.

Privacy Addendum for Job Applicants
This addendum (‘Addendum’) to our Meredith IR Privacy Statement applies to job applicants.
It describes the personal information that Meredith Corporation, its subsidiaries and affiliates, collect during our application and recruitment process and how we use it.
This notice applies solely to the personal information we collect in the course of your employment recruitment process.
What Personal Information Do We Collect?
We may collect, store, and use the following types of personal information about job applicants:
Contact Information.
We collect contact information such as your name, telephone number, and email address.
Application Information.
We collected application information from your resume and application form, which will likely include details about your qualifications, skills, experience and employment history; references supplied by your former employer, work permit/visa application information, etc.
==========
Content of Interviews.
We collect personal information from the interviews and phone-screenings you may have, if any.
==========
Employment/Job Preferences.
We collect details about the type of employment you are or may be looking for, current and/or desired salary and other terms relating to compensation and benefits packages, willingness to relocate, or other job preferences.
==========
Protected Classifications under the Law.
We collect information about your ethnic origin, sexual orientation, health and religion or belief, disability status, etc. for equal opportunity monitoring and reporting, purely on a voluntary basis.
==========
Reference/Background Check Results.
We collect reference information and/or other personal information received from background checks (where applicable), including personal information provided by third parties.
Any Other Personal Information You Provide Us in the Context of Job Recruiting.
We collect personal information in order to administer aspects of your recruitment (or employment) or to comply with legal obligations or company policies.
How Do We Use Your Personal Information?
We use your personal information in order to carry out our application and recruitment process, including:
To assess your skills, qualifications, and interests against our career opportunities.
To verify your personal information and carry out reference checks and/or conducting background checks (where applicable) if you are offered a job.
To communicate with you about the recruitment process and/or your application(s), including, in appropriate cases, informing you of other potential career opportunities.
To assist you with obtaining an immigration visa or work permit where required (upon request).
To ensure meaningful equal opportunity monitoring and reporting.
==========
To comply with applicable laws, regulations, subpoenas, legal processes or enforceable governmental requests.
If you are offered and accept employment with us, the personal information collected during the application and recruitment process will become part of your employment record.
==========
We may also use the personal information that we collect for legitimate business purposes, such as: assessing or executing mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, or business transfers; managing business tools and information technology systems, including computers, mobile phones provided by the company, mobile phones you register under a Bring Your Own Device policy (if applicable), Internet and software access, email accounts, etc.; managing disputes and disciplinary proceedings; and contacting you or your household when needed.
Who May Have Access to Your Personal Information?
Your personal information may be shared with:
Our affiliates, subsidiaries, or joint ventures.
Your personal information may be shared with our affiliates, subsidiaries or joint ventures in relation to the purposes described above.
If you have been referred for a job by a current employee, with your consent, we may inform that employee about the progress of your application and let the employee know the outcome of the process.
==========
Service Providers.
We may also use service providers acting on our behalf to perform some of the services described above including for the purposes of the verification / background checks.
These service providers may be located outside the country in which you live or the country where the position you have applied for is located.
==========
Third Parties.
We may also share your personal information with third parties if we have your consent; for example, if you have given us permission to contact your references.
We may also share your personal information in our efforts to detect, prevent or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues, or to protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of Meredith, our consumers, employees, or the public or as otherwise required by law.
==========
Legal Compliance, Business Transfers, and Other Disclosures.
We may be required to share your personal information with applicable government agencies, courts, law enforcement, or others.
==========
Changes to this Privacy Statement
We may update this Privacy Addendum for Job Applicants from time to time.
If we do so, we will make an updated copy available.
