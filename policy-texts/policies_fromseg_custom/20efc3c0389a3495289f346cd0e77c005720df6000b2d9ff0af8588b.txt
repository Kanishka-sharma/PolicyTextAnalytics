WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
When you view an ad on a site or app which uses our services, we collect information about the ad that you have viewed.
This information does not enable us to work out your identity in real life, but does enable us to recognise from which advertisements you are coming from.
We may specifically collect the information about the ad you have viewed or clicked
We collect some or all of the following information about the ad you have viewed, clicked: (i) the content type of the ad (what the ad is about, e.g. games, finance, entertainment, news); (ii) the ad type (e.g. whether the ad is a text, image, richmedia or video based ad); (iii) where the ad is being served (e.g. the address of the website on which the ad appears); and (iv) certain information about post-click activity in relation to the ad (e.g. whether you proceed to fill-in form, purchase the product or service advertised).

COOKIES / FINGER PRINT
We only use a session cookie to track from which format, publisher, ad network, the user is coming to view a mobile website.
The cookie only contains a unique trackid per format, publishers and ad networks and will expire after 20 minutes.
In certain instances, S4M may use HTML5 storage for enabling, tracking functionalities within an ad or for counting purposes.
This can be removed by clearing your browser’s cache.
==========
In other instances, Fusio solution may use all public information provided by users’ navigation :
User-Agent : String sent to web server which specifies client software navigation, smartphone’s OS version
Accept-Encoding : data which indicates if the request support encodings
Accept-language : indicates what are the acceptable languages for response
Accept-charset : indicates what are the character sets that are acceptable
 
Fusio reconciles those data to track the user along his ads-navigation :
Download of an application.
In particular, Fusio compares data collected from the advertising to data collected from the first launch of an application (made easier by the use of our iOS/Android SDK) and reproduced navigation sessions per track code (used in all ads) to know exactly where the customer comes from.
