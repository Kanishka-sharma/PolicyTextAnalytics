What does this Privacy Policy cover?
This Privacy Policy applies to collection, use, storage and disclosure of information by InternetFuel.

InternetFuel does not determine which information will be collected by that third party.
What information does InternetFuel collect and how is it used?
InternetFuel may collect any or all of the following three types of information: (a) traffic or click-through data, (b) cookies, or (c) contact information and other personally identifying information.
InternetFuel 's collection and use of each type of data is described below, as well as the ways (if any) in which the data may be shared with third parties.
(a) Traffic or Click-Through Data.
==========
Data about traffic and usage is not personally identifying or identifiable, and is not supplemented or combined with personally identifying or identifiable information.
InternetFuel may share information it collects with certain selected third parties, but only in aggregated, anonymous form.
For example, we may tell an advertiser that a certain number of people visited a certain area on our Web site.
==========
InternetFuel collects some information regarding use of the Services by observing browser functions and the files (pages) users access.
For example, InternetFuel 's servers collect information about the site from which you entered to enable InternetFuel to calculate and pay its affiliates, information about the most frequently searched terms to help its advertisers provide the most relevant search results, information about usage to check for fraudulent behavior.
(b) Cookies.
InternetFuel collects information about online behavior using cookies.
These cookies contain identification information that enables us to see how our users are interacting with our site and how frequently they are returning.
==========
InternetFuel cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information.
Cookies lets us know about repeat visitors, InternetFuel may also use cookies to identify which sites our repeat visitors are coming from, so that we can place more of our online advertising on these sites, and can, where necessary, pay our affiliates and other business partners.
You may set your browser software to reject cookies, but you may not be able to use all of the features of InternetFuel.
InternetFuel may share information about usage of the Services with third parties, but only in aggregated, anonymous form.
(c) Contact Information or Personally Identifying Information.
==========
InternetFuel can obtain access to your name, e-mail address and other contact information only (1) when you become an advertiser/merchant with InternetFuel, provide comments, submit a problem, or request information from InternetFuel and provide your name or return contact information, or when you register for the advanced features of InternetFuel.
Contact information provided in connection with problems, comments or requests is used only to respond to those problems, comments or requests.
This information is not stored or used in connection with traffic or click-through data and is provided to a third party only as necessary to respond to the comment, problem or request.
==========
During the registration process, you may be asked to provide certain information, including contact information and other personally identifying information.
InternetFuel will use registration information to provide updates, and other business purposes related to making available the features and functions of InternetFuel.
InternetFuel may use registration information to make users aware of new products or special offers or promotions.
==========
InternetFuel will abide by requests from you (in accordance with the procedure below) to discontinue such use.
InternetFuel stores personally identifiable information obtained through registration securely (as described below) and considers it part of the assets of its business.
InternetFuel may provide certain registration information to third parties for use in marketing and in designing or providing their own services.
Is data stored by InternetFuel secure?
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of information, InternetFuel has put in place physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects online.

You should consider any communication that you transmit to InternetFuel (such as data, questions or answers, comments, or suggestions) as non-confidential, and agree that InternetFuel will not be liable or responsible if information that belongs to you is intercepted and used by an unintended recipient.
Does InternetFuel collect personally identifying information from or about children?
InternetFuel uses reasonable efforts not to collect, use or disclose individually identifiable information from children under the age of 13.
The Services are not targeted at children, and children may not register for any portions of the Services that require registration.
==========
How can I access or correct any personally identifying information about me?
To request a copy of the personally identifiable information you have sent to InternetFuel, please send an email containing your request to InternetFuel@InternetFuel.com.
You must provide your full name and the e-mail from which the request is received (and to which the response will be sent) must match the e-mail on file with InternetFuel.
You may then correct or delete such information using the procedures specified in the e-mail.
You may also go to the http://admin.InternetFuel.com and modify personally identifying information by entering your password and accessing the "Update Profile" section of the site.
