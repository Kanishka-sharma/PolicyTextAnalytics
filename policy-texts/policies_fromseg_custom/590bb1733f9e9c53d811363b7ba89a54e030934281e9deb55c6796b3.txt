Payment Methods

AGE LIMIT

Refunds and Exchanges
Before purchasing tickets, carefully review your booking details.

If the event was moved or rescheduled, the venue or promoter may set refund limitations.

Please make sure to include/provide your account number so we can expedite your refund if applicable.

Orders will be processed only after a billing address, and other billing information, has been verified.
