Introduction: what is a cookie?
This Cookie Policy explains when and why cookies may be set when using the Blue Billywig website.
"Cookies" are text files that are stored in your browsers storage to keep track of data.
Thanks to cookies, we can remember important information and facilitate your use of the Blue Billywig website.
A cookie typically contains the name of the domain from which the cookie originated, the 'lifespan' of the cookie and a randomly generated unique value.
There are different types of cookies:
Session cookies are temporary cookies that are kept in the cookie database of your browser until you leave the website.
Permanent cookies remain stored in the cookie database of your browser for much longer (the duration depends on the lifetime of the specific cookie).
==========
When we use session cookies to track the total number of visitors to our website, we use anonymous, collected data (the cookies themselves do not contain any personal data).
We may also use cookies to remember your computer and customize your online experience when you visit the website again later.
We can link personal data to a cookie file in such cases.
