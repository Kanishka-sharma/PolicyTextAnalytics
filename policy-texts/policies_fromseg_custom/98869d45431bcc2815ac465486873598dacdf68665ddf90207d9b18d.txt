Use of Cookies and Web Beacons

				When you view our website we might store some information on your computer.
This
				information will be in the form of a "cookie" or similar file.
Cookies are small
				pieces of information stored on your hard drive, not on our site.
Cookies do not
				spy on you or otherwise invade your privacy, and they cannot invade your hard
				drive and steal information.
They are designed to help you navigate our website as
				easily as possible.
==========

In the course of displaying our advertisements to you, a unique
				third-party cookie may be placed or recognized on your browser.
In addition, we
				use web beacons to help manage our online advertising.
