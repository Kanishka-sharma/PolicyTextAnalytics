AdventNet Privacy Policy
AdventNet is committed to respecting your online privacy 
                    and recognize your need for appropriate protection of any 
                    information you share with us.
You can visit most pages on 
                    our site without giving us any information about yourself.
==========
But sometimes we do need information to provide services that 
                    you request.
Use of your Personal Information
We use your personal information for the following purposes:

To ensure our site is relevant to your needs.
To deliver services, such as newsletters, events, training 
                      or software, that you request or purchase.
To help us create and publish content most relevant to 
                      you.
==========
To alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated 
                      information and other new services from AdventNet, if you 
                      so request.
Use of Cookies
When you visit our site, a cookie is placed on your machine 
                    or is read if you have visited the site previously.
Cookies 
                    are used to remember the information provided by you so that 
                    you do not have to fill it again when you download the same 
                    product or any other AdventNet product.
Cookies will be placed 
                    only for the specified purpose and your information will not 
                    be used otherwise.
You have the option to disable cookies 
                    on your computer.
Security of your Personal Information 
Your personal information and other information related to 
                    your online activity at the AdventNet store will not be given 
                    to any outside organization for any purpose, except that certain 
                    information may be shared with our partners only for purposes 
                    described in 'Use of your Personal Information' above.
==========
For 
                    example, if you request that a sales person contact you, we 
                    may pass your request to a local reseller partner, if that 
                    is appropriate.
In no case do we sell your information.
==========
Internet Commerce
The online store at store.adventnet.com is designed to give 
                    you options concerning the privacy of your personal information.
Credit card information submitted for your purchase is never 
                    remembered by our site.
AdventNet is committed to data security 
                    with respect to information collected on our site.
==========
We offer 
                    the industry standard security measures available through 
                    your browser called SSL encryption.
We use VeriSign Digital 
                    Certificates for SSL Encryption to make sure that all transactions 
                    are secure.
If at any time you would like to make a purchase, 
                    but do not want to provide your credit card information online, 
                    you may contact sales.
==========
